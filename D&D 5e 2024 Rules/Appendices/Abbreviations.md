# Abbreviations

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Common abbreviations used throughout D&D 5e rules and character sheets.

---

## Ability Scores

| Abbreviation | Full Name | Primary Uses |
|--------------|-----------|--------------|
| **STR** | Strength | Melee attacks, carrying capacity, Athletics |
| **DEX** | Dexterity | AC, ranged attacks, initiative, Stealth |
| **CON** | Constitution | HP, concentration checks |
| **INT** | Intelligence | Wizard spellcasting, Investigation |
| **WIS** | Wisdom | Cleric/Druid spellcasting, Perception |
| **CHA** | Charisma | Bard/Sorcerer/Warlock spellcasting, social skills |

---

## Combat Terms

| Abbreviation | Full Name | Description |
|--------------|-----------|-------------|
| **AC** | Armor Class | Target number to hit with an attack |
| **HP** | Hit Points | Measure of health/vitality |
| **temp HP** | Temporary Hit Points | Bonus HP that don't stack and go away first |
| **HD** | Hit Dice | Dice used for HP and healing during short rests |
| **PB** | Proficiency Bonus | Bonus added for proficient skills/attacks/saves |
| **DC** | Difficulty Class | Target number for ability checks and saves |
| **d20** | Twenty-sided die | Used for attacks, saves, and checks |
| **NAT 20** | Natural 20 | Rolling a 20 on the die (critical hit/success) |
| **NAT 1** | Natural 1 | Rolling a 1 on the die (automatic miss) |
| **AOE** | Area of Effect | Spells/abilities that affect an area |
| **DPR** | Damage Per Round | Average damage dealt each round |
| **AoO** | Attack of Opportunity | Reaction attack when enemy leaves reach |
