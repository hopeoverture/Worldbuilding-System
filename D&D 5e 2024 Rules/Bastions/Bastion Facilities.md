## Detailed Facility Descriptions

### Arcane Study

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** Ability to cast at least one spell
**Hirelings:** Apprentice arcanist, scribe

An arcane study is a dedicated space for magical research and crafting. It contains a workbench, reference materials, component storage, and magical focusing tools.

**Orders:**
- **Craft Scroll**: Create a spell scroll (time and cost based on spell level)
- **Research Spell**: Attempt to learn a new spell or identify magical effects
- **Arcane Experiment**: Attempt to create a minor magical effect (DM discretion)

**Enlarged Benefits:** Reduced crafting time, ability to craft higher-level scrolls, expanded component storage.

---

### Armory

**Size:** Cramped (Roomy when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Armorer, weapon keeper

An armory stores, maintains, and organizes weapons and armor. Well-maintained equipment performs better; neglected gear can fail at crucial moments.

**Orders:**
- **Maintain Equipment**: Keep stored weapons and armor in perfect condition
- **Trade Weapons**: Buy or sell equipment through hireling contacts
- **Prepare for Battle**: Ready specific equipment loadouts for quick access

**Enlarged Benefits:** Larger storage capacity, ability to maintain magical equipment, access to rarer weapons through trade networks.

---

### Barracks

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Sergeant, guards (1d6+2)

A barracks houses trained defenders who protect the bastion and can assist with military matters.

**Orders:**
- **Defend**: Guards actively patrol and defend the bastion
- **Recruit**: Seek additional defenders (costs gold)
- **Patrol Area**: Extend protection to surrounding region
- **Escort**: Guards accompany character on local travel

**Guard Statistics:**
- Use **Guard** stat block (MM)
- Roomy barracks: 1d6+2 guards
- Vast barracks: 2d6+4 guards

**Enlarged Benefits:** More guards, higher-quality defenders (veterans), fortification improvements.

---

### Gaming Hall

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Croupier, bouncer, servers

A gaming hall offers games of chance and skill, attracting visitors and generating income through house advantage.

**Orders:**
- **Run Games**: Generate gold (1d6 × 10 gp per Bastion Turn, variable)
- **High Stakes Night**: Risk more for potentially greater returns
- **Information Gathering**: Patrons sometimes share valuable information

**Enlarged Benefits:** Increased income potential (1d8 × 10 gp), VIP room attracts wealthy patrons, improved security.

---

### Garden

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged, becomes Greenhouse)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Gardener, herbalist

A garden grows useful plants—herbs, vegetables, and medicinal plants that can be used for cooking, alchemy, or sale.

**Orders:**
- **Harvest**: Collect mature plants (herbs worth 25 gp per Bastion Turn)
- **Cultivate**: Focus on growing specific plants
- **Potion Garden**: Grow potion ingredients (provides components for 1 potion per Bastion Turn)

**Enlarged Benefits:** Becomes a Greenhouse—can grow rare and exotic plants, increased yield.

---

### Greenhouse

**Size:** Vast
**Prerequisite:** Garden facility (enlarged) or direct purchase
**Hirelings:** Master botanist, assistants

A greenhouse is an enclosed garden capable of growing plants from any climate, including rare and magical specimens.

**Orders:**
- **Cultivate Rare Plants**: Grow plants not native to the region
- **Exotic Harvest**: Produce rare ingredients (worth 50+ gp per Bastion Turn)
- **Magical Cultivation**: Attempt to grow plants with magical properties (DM discretion)

---

### Guildhall

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** Membership in a guild or organization
**Hirelings:** Steward, attendants

A guildhall provides meeting space for an organization the character belongs to, strengthening their position within it.

**Orders:**
- **Host Meeting**: Convene guild business (may provide favors or information)
- **Recruit Members**: Expand the organization
- **Guild Business**: Pursue organization-specific activities

**Benefits:** Improved standing in the organization, access to guild resources, networking opportunities.

**Enlarged Benefits:** Can host multiple organizations, improved facilities attract higher-ranking members.

---

### Library

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Librarian, scribes

A library contains books, scrolls, and documents on various subjects. It's invaluable for research and education.

**Orders:**
- **Research Topic**: Gain advantage on Intelligence checks related to a topic researched
- **Study**: Character gains proficiency in a skill or tool (requires extended time)
- **Acquire Texts**: Expand the collection on specific subjects

**Enlarged Benefits:** Larger collection provides bonuses to more obscure topics, rare book access, can attract scholars.

---

### Meditation Chamber

**Size:** Cramped (Roomy when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Attendant

A meditation chamber is a quiet, serene space designed for contemplation, mental focus, and spiritual connection.

**Orders:**
- **Meditate**: Regain one spent class resource (Ki, Sorcery Points, etc.) during downtime
- **Commune**: Attempt to receive insight or guidance (DM determines results)
- **Center Self**: Gain advantage on Wisdom saving throws for the next adventure

**Enlarged Benefits:** Can accommodate group meditation, enhanced spiritual connection, faster recovery.

---

### Menagerie

**Size:** Vast
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Beast keeper, handlers

A menagerie houses exotic creatures, unusual mounts, and animal companions. It requires significant space and specialized care.

**Orders:**
- **Tend Creatures**: Maintain the health and wellbeing of housed creatures
- **Train Beast**: Work with a creature to improve its behavior or capabilities
- **Breed**: Attempt to breed housed creatures (long-term process)
- **Acquire Creature**: Seek out and obtain new specimens (costs vary)

**Special:** Some creatures may require additional facilities (aquatic tank, aviary, etc.).

---

### Observatory

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Astronomer, assistant

An observatory contains telescopes and instruments for studying the heavens. It aids navigation, timekeeping, and certain divinations.

**Orders:**
- **Chart Stars**: Gain advantage on navigation checks for upcoming travel
- **Observe Omens**: Attempt to gain insight into future events
- **Track Celestial Event**: Monitor for specific astronomical phenomena

**Enlarged Benefits:** More powerful instruments, ability to predict events further in advance, magical astronomy.

---

### Pub

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Barkeep, servers, cook

A pub serves food and drink to visitors, generating income and serving as a gathering place for information.

**Orders:**
- **Operate**: Generate steady income (2d6 × 5 gp per Bastion Turn)
- **Gather Rumors**: Patrons share information—learn local news and gossip
- **Host Event**: Special events attract specific clientele

**Enlarged Benefits:** Increased income (2d6 × 10 gp), attracts more notable patrons, private rooms for discreet meetings.

---

### Sacristy

**Size:** Cramped (Roomy when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** Devotion to a deity or religious practice
**Hirelings:** Acolyte

A sacristy stores holy items, vestments, and religious objects. It serves as a preparation space for religious ceremonies.

**Orders:**
- **Consecrate Item**: Bless items for religious use
- **Prepare Ritual**: Ready components for religious ceremonies
- **Maintain Holy Items**: Keep sacred objects in proper condition

**Enlarged Benefits:** Can store more powerful holy items, attract visiting clergy, enhance religious rituals.

---

### Sanctuary

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** Ability to cast healing magic OR devotion to a healing deity
**Hirelings:** Healer, attendants

A sanctuary is a place of healing and protection. It provides care for the wounded and can be warded against intrusion.

**Orders:**
- **Heal**: Provide care for injured creatures (characters regain extra Hit Dice during rest)
- **Ward**: Place protective magic on the facility
- **Tend the Sick**: Cure diseases or remove curses (requires character involvement)

**Enlarged Benefits:** Can care for more patients, stronger wards, attract those seeking healing (potential allies or plot hooks).

---

### Scriptorium

**Size:** Cramped (Roomy when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Scribe

A scriptorium is dedicated to the creation and copying of documents. It can produce official papers, copy texts, or create forgeries.

**Orders:**
- **Copy Text**: Duplicate a book or document (1-4 weeks depending on length)
- **Create Documents**: Produce official-looking papers (letters, credentials)
- **Forge**: Create false documents (requires materials, risk of detection)

**Enlarged Benefits:** Faster copying, multiple simultaneous projects, better quality forgeries.

---

### Smithy

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Smith, apprentice

A smithy contains a forge, anvil, and tools for metalworking. It can produce weapons, armor, and other metal goods.

**Orders:**
- **Craft Weapon/Armor**: Create nonmagical weapons or armor (time and cost per item)
- **Repair**: Fix damaged equipment
- **Customize**: Modify existing equipment (add features, resize)
- **Masterwork**: Attempt to create superior-quality items (DM discretion)

**Enlarged Benefits:** Can work with exotic materials, faster crafting, ability to assist with magical item creation.

---

### Stable

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Stablehand, groom

A stable houses and cares for horses and other mounts. Well-tended mounts perform better and stay healthier.

**Orders:**
- **Tend Mounts**: Keep housed mounts healthy and ready
- **Train Mount**: Improve a mount's capabilities or behavior
- **Acquire Mount**: Purchase or trade for new mounts
- **Breed**: Attempt to breed housed mounts (long-term)

**Enlarged Benefits:** House more mounts, accommodate exotic mounts (griffons, etc.), faster training.

---

### Storehouse

**Size:** Vast
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Warehouse master, laborers

A storehouse provides bulk storage for goods, equipment, and trade materials. It can generate income through trading.

**Orders:**
- **Store**: Safely keep large quantities of goods
- **Trade**: Buy low, sell high—generate income from market fluctuations (3d6 × 10 gp potential per Bastion Turn, but variable)
- **Supply**: Provide equipment for expeditions from stored goods

**Special:** Trade income is variable and can result in losses during bad markets.

---

### Theater

**Size:** Vast
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Director, performers, stage crew

A theater hosts performances that entertain, inspire, and spread reputation. It can be a source of income and influence.

**Orders:**
- **Perform**: Host a show (generates 2d6 × 10 gp, spreads reputation)
- **Inspire**: Dedicated performance grants Bardic Inspiration-like benefits to attendees
- **Commission**: Create a performance about specific events (propaganda, history)

**Special:** Theater requires ongoing investment in costumes, scripts, and talent to maintain quality.

---

### Training Area

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Training master

A training area provides space and equipment for combat practice and physical training.

**Orders:**
- **Train**: Practice combat techniques—gain advantage on first attack roll in next combat
- **Spar**: Practice against an opponent (training master or visitor)
- **Teach**: Train others in combat techniques
- **Condition**: Physical training provides temporary HP for next adventure

**Enlarged Benefits:** More training equipment, can train multiple people, attract skilled trainers.

---

### Trophy Room

**Size:** Cramped (Roomy when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** Defeated foes worth displaying
**Hirelings:** Curator

A trophy room displays evidence of victories—monster heads, captured banners, recovered artifacts.

**Orders:**
- **Display**: Arrange trophies for maximum impact
- **Intimidate**: Visitors must make Wisdom saves or be frightened
- **Catalog**: Document conquests for historical record
- **Identify**: Study monster trophies to learn weaknesses

**Enlarged Benefits:** Room for more trophies, enhanced intimidation effect, attracts those impressed by deeds.

---

### War Room

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Strategist, scouts

A war room contains maps, strategic materials, and planning resources for military operations.

**Orders:**
- **Plan Campaign**: Gain advantage on tactical decisions in upcoming military engagement
- **Gather Intelligence**: Scouts collect information about enemies or regions
- **Coordinate**: Organize multiple groups or allies for combined operations
- **Analyze Enemy**: Study a specific foe to identify weaknesses

**Enlarged Benefits:** Better maps and intelligence, can coordinate larger forces, attract military advisors.

---

### Workshop

**Size:** Roomy (Vast when enlarged)
**Prerequisite:** None
**Hirelings:** Craftsman, apprentice

A workshop provides tools and space for crafting mundane items—furniture, tools, clothing, and other goods.

**Orders:**
- **Craft Item**: Create nonmagical items (time and cost based on complexity)
- **Repair**: Fix damaged mundane items
- **Customize**: Modify existing items
- **Commission**: Create items for sale (generates 1d6 × 10 gp per Bastion Turn)

**Enlarged Benefits:** Faster crafting, can work with unusual materials, ability to create more complex items.
