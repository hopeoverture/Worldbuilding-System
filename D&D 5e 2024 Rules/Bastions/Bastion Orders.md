# Bastion Orders

Orders are commands given to bastion facilities during a Bastion Turn. Each special facility can receive one order per Bastion Turn.

## Order Timing

- **Bastion Turn**: Occurs every 7 days of in-game time
- **Orders Issued**: At the start of each Bastion Turn
- **Results**: Available at the end of the Bastion Turn (or when character returns)
- **Character Presence**: Some orders require the character to be present

## Special Order Combinations

Some orders from different facilities work together:

| Facilities | Combined Order | Effect |
|------------|----------------|--------|
| Library + Arcane Study | Deep Research | Reduced spell research time |
| Smithy + Armory | Full Maintenance | All equipment gains +1 condition |
| Garden + Sanctuary | Healing Garden | Enhanced healing effect |
| War Room + Barracks | Coordinated Defense | Guards gain +2 AC during attacks |
| Pub + Gaming Hall | Full Entertainment | Combined income + extra rumors |
