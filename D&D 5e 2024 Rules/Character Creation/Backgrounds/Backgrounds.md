# Backgrounds

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Backgrounds define your character's history and training before becoming an adventurer. In 2024, backgrounds provide ability score increases, skill and tool proficiencies, and an Origin Feat.

---

## What Backgrounds Provide

Every background grants:
- **Ability Score Increases**: +2 to one score and +1 to another (or +1 to three scores)
- **Skill Proficiencies**: Two skills
- **Tool Proficiency**: One tool
- **Starting Equipment**: Specific gear worth 50 GP
- **Origin Feat**: A feat from the Origin Feat category

## Creating Custom Backgrounds

With DM permission, you can create a custom background:

1. Choose any **two skills**
2. Choose any **one tool**
3. Choose any **Origin Feat** you qualify for
4. Assign ability scores: +2/+1 (two scores) or +1/+1/+1 (three scores)
5. Create 50 GP worth of starting equipment

---

## Background and Roleplaying

Each background suggests:
- **Personality Traits**: Distinctive behaviors
- **Ideals**: Core beliefs that drive you
- **Bonds**: Connections to people, places, or things
- **Flaws**: Weaknesses or vices

Use these as inspiration, not requirements.
