# Aasimar

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Aasimar are humanoids with celestial heritage, touched by divine power. They possess innate healing abilities and can transform into radiant or shadowy forms.

---

## Basic Traits

| Trait | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Creature Type** | Humanoid, Celestial |
| **Size** | Medium (4-7 feet tall) |
| **Speed** | 30 feet |
| **Lifespan** | ~160 years |

---

## Racial Traits

### Celestial Resistance
You have resistance to **Necrotic** and **Radiant** damage.

### Darkvision
You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You discern colors in that darkness only as shades of gray.

### Healing Hands
As a **Magic action**, you can touch a creature and roll a number of d4s equal to your Proficiency Bonus. The creature regains hit points equal to the total rolled.

Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a **Long Rest**.

### Light Bearer
You know the **Light** cantrip. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for it.

### Celestial Revelation (3rd Level)
When you reach 3rd level, choose one of the following transformations. You can use this transformation once per Long Rest, and it lasts for 1 minute or until you end it (no action required).

#### Necrotic Shroud
Your eyes briefly become pools of darkness, and ghostly flightless wings sprout from your back momentarily.
- Creatures within 10 feet must succeed on a **Charisma saving throw** (DC = 8 + Proficiency + CHA modifier) or be **Frightened** of you until the end of your next turn.
- Once per turn when you deal damage, you deal extra **Necrotic damage** equal to your Proficiency Bonus.

#### Radiant Consumption
Searing light temporarily radiates from your eyes and mouth.
- At the end of each of your turns, each creature within 10 feet takes **Radiant damage** equal to your Proficiency Bonus.
- Once per turn when you deal damage, you deal extra **Radiant damage** equal to your Proficiency Bonus.

#### Radiant Soul
Two luminous, spectral wings sprout from your back temporarily.
- You have a **Fly Speed** equal to your walking speed.
- Once per turn when you deal damage, you deal extra **Radiant damage** equal to your Proficiency Bonus.

## Roleplaying an Aasimar

### Physical Appearance
- Often striking beauty with an otherworldly quality
- May have unusual eye colors (silver, gold, pupil-less white)
- Sometimes have faint patterns on skin that glow softly
- Hair might shimmer or move as if in a gentle breeze

### Personality Considerations
- May feel a sense of destiny or purpose
- Could struggle with the weight of expectations
- Might be drawn to help others or fight evil
- Some embrace their heritage; others hide it

### Background Ideas
- Raised in a temple, trained for divine service
- Hidden among common folk, unaware of true nature until recently
- Fleeing from those who would exploit celestial blood
- Searching for answers about missing celestial guide
