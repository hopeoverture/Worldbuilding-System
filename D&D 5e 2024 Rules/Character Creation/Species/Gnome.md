# Gnome

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Gnomes are small, clever humanoids with a natural resistance to magic. They possess innate curiosity and come in two lineages: Forest and Rock.

---

## Basic Traits

| Trait | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Creature Type** | Humanoid |
| **Size** | Small (3-4 feet tall) |
| **Speed** | 30 feet |
| **Lifespan** | ~425 years |

---

## Racial Traits

### Darkvision
You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You discern colors in that darkness only as shades of gray.

### Gnomish Cunning
You have **Advantage** on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.

### Gnomish Lineage (1st Level)
Choose one of the following lineages.

---

## Gnomish Lineages

### Forest Gnome

**Minor Illusion Cantrip**: You know the *Minor Illusion* cantrip. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for it.

**Speak with Small Beasts**: Through sounds and gestures, you can communicate simple ideas with Small or smaller Beasts.

### Rock Gnome

**Artificer's Lore**: Whenever you make a History check related to magic items, alchemical objects, or technological devices, you can add twice your Proficiency Bonus instead of any Proficiency Bonus you normally apply.

**Tinker**: You know the *Mending* and *Prestidigitation* cantrips. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for them.

---

## Trait Analysis

### Gnomish Cunning
This is one of the strongest defensive traits in the game:
- Advantage on INT saves (mind flayers, feeblemind)
- Advantage on WIS saves (charm, fear, most spells)
- Advantage on CHA saves (banishment, some fear effects)

Most save-or-suck spells target these abilities. Gnomes are exceptionally resistant to mental manipulation.

### Size: Small
- Can move through larger creatures' spaces
- Can fit in smaller areas
- No penalties to weapon use in 2024 (small creatures can use heavy weapons)
- Speed is 30 feet (changed from 25 feet in 2014)

### Lineage Comparison

| Feature | Forest Gnome | Rock Gnome |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| Cantrips | Minor Illusion | Mending, Prestidigitation |
| Special | Speak with Small Beasts | Artificer's Lore (double proficiency) |
| Best For | Illusion-focused, nature | Crafting, investigation, utility |

## Roleplaying a Gnome

### Physical Appearance
- Small stature with large, bright eyes
- Often have colorful hair or unusual features
- Animated expressions and gestures
- May favor practical but whimsical clothing

### Personality Considerations
- Intense curiosity about how things work
- Love of invention, tinkering, or nature
- Optimistic outlook despite challenges
- May become obsessively focused on interests

### Background Ideas
- Inventor seeking to test a revolutionary device
- Naturalist documenting the world's creatures
- Refugee from a gnomish community destroyed by disaster
- Spy using small size and illusions to gather information

### Cultural Notes
- Often live in hidden communities
- Value knowledge, creativity, and ingenuity
- Rock Gnomes: Fond of mechanisms and gadgets
- Forest Gnomes: Connected to woodland creatures

## Optimization Notes

- Gnomish Cunning is arguably the best defensive species trait
- INT-based classes benefit most from the racial cantrips
- Great for any character who fears save-or-suck effects
- Consider for fragile characters (Wizard, Sorcerer) who need save protection
