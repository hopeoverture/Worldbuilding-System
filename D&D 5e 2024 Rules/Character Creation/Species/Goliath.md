# Goliath

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Goliaths are towering humanoids with stone-like skin, adapted to high mountain life. They possess remarkable endurance and can temporarily grow to Large size.

---

## Basic Traits

| Trait | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Creature Type** | Humanoid |
| **Size** | Medium (7-8 feet tall) |
| **Speed** | 35 feet |
| **Lifespan** | ~80 years |

---

## Racial Traits

### Large Form
Starting at 5th level, as a **Bonus Action**, you can change your size to **Large** for 10 minutes. This change lasts until you use a Bonus Action to end it or until you are incapacitated.

While Large:
- You have **Advantage** on Strength checks
- Your Speed increases by 10 feet

Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a **Long Rest**.

### Powerful Build
You count as **one size larger** when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift.

### Giant Ancestry
You gain one of the following traits of your choice:

#### Cloud's Jaunt (Cloud Giant)
As a **Bonus Action**, you can magically teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see.

You can use this trait a number of times equal to your **Proficiency Bonus**, regaining all uses on a **Long Rest**.

#### Fire's Burn (Fire Giant)
When you hit a target with an attack roll and deal damage, you can also deal **1d10 Fire damage**.

You can use this trait a number of times equal to your **Proficiency Bonus**, regaining all uses on a **Long Rest**.

#### Frost's Chill (Frost Giant)
When you hit a target with an attack roll and deal damage, you can also deal **1d6 Cold damage**. The target's Speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.

You can use this trait a number of times equal to your **Proficiency Bonus**, regaining all uses on a **Long Rest**.

#### Hill's Tumble (Hill Giant)
When you hit a Large or smaller creature with an attack roll and deal damage, you can knock that target **Prone**.

You can use this trait a number of times equal to your **Proficiency Bonus**, regaining all uses on a **Long Rest**.

#### Stone's Endurance (Stone Giant)
When you take damage, you can use your **Reaction** to roll 1d12 + your Constitution modifier and reduce the damage by that amount.

You can use this trait a number of times equal to your **Proficiency Bonus**, regaining all uses on a **Long Rest**.

#### Storm's Thunder (Storm Giant)
When you take damage from a creature within 60 feet, you can use your **Reaction** to deal **1d8 Thunder damage** to that creature.

You can use this trait a number of times equal to your **Proficiency Bonus**, regaining all uses on a **Long Rest**.

## Giant Ancestry Comparison

| Ancestry | Type | Trigger | Effect |
|----------|------|---------|--------|
| Cloud | Bonus Action | Anytime | Teleport 30 ft. |
| Fire | On Hit | Deal damage | +1d10 Fire |
| Frost | On Hit | Deal damage | +1d6 Cold, slow target |
| Hill | On Hit | Deal damage | Knock Prone |
| Stone | Reaction | Take damage | Reduce damage |
| Storm | Reaction | Take damage | 1d8 Thunder to attacker |

### Recommended Uses
- **Cloud**: Best mobility; excellent for any class
- **Fire**: Highest damage; best for consistent attackers
- **Frost**: Control + damage; good for martial classes
- **Hill**: Tactical control; synergizes with Extra Attack
- **Stone**: Best defense; essential for tanks
- **Storm**: Deterrent; good for characters who get hit often

## Trait Analysis

### 35 ft. Speed
- Fastest base walking speed of any species
- Helps close distance with enemies
- Better for melee characters

### Large Form (5th Level)
- Advantage on Strength checks (grapples, Athletics)
- Additional 10 ft. Speed (45 ft. total!)
- Can occupy 10×10 ft. space
- May have issues with tight dungeon corridors

### Powerful Build
- Carry, push, drag as if Large
- Useful for hauling treasure, moving objects
- No combat benefit, but great utility

## Roleplaying a Goliath

### Physical Appearance
- Towering height (7-8 feet)
- Gray or stone-colored skin with darker patches
- Bald, often with tattooed patterns
- Muscular, athletic build

### Personality Considerations
- May value competition and self-sufficiency
- Often straightforward and honest
- Could measure themselves against others constantly
- Might struggle with concepts of wealth or status

### Background Ideas
- Exiled from tribe for losing a competition
- Wanderer seeking worthy challenges
- Guardian of a remote mountain pass
- Last survivor of a tribe destroyed by giants

## Combat Tips

- **Stone's Endurance** is best used against big hits—don't waste on chip damage
- **Large Form** at 5th level makes you dominant in grapples
- Combine **Hill's Tumble** with Extra Attack for repeated prone attempts
- **Cloud's Jaunt** teleport has no verbal/somatic components—works while grappled
- Your 35 ft. speed lets you reach enemies faster than most allies
