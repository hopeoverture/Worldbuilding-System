# Orc

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Orcs are powerful humanoids with superior darkvision and remarkable resilience. They can push themselves with adrenaline-fueled bursts and survive killing blows.

## Racial Traits

### Adrenaline Rush
You can take the **Dash** action as a **Bonus Action**. When you do so, you gain a number of **Temporary Hit Points** equal to your Proficiency Bonus.

You can use this trait a number of times equal to your **Proficiency Bonus**, regaining all uses on a **Short or Long Rest**.

### Darkvision
You can see in dim light within **120 feet** as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You discern colors in that darkness only as shades of gray.

### Relentless Endurance
When you are reduced to **0 hit points** but not killed outright, you can drop to **1 hit point** instead.

Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a **Long Rest**.

---

## Trait Analysis

### Adrenaline Rush
Extremely versatile mobility trait:
- **Dash as a Bonus Action**: Double your movement when needed
- **Temp HP**: Small buffer, scales with level
- **Multiple Uses**: Equal to Proficiency Bonus (2-6 per rest)
- **Recharges on Short Rest**: Available frequently

**Use Cases**:
- Close distance to ranged enemies
- Retreat from dangerous situations
- Reach downed allies quickly
- Begin combat with a burst of speed

### Darkvision (120 ft.)
- Double the range of most species (60 ft.)
- Exceptional for underground or night operations
- Can spot threats before they spot you
- Pairs with stealth for night ambushes

### Relentless Endurance
One of the best survival traits:
- Prevents going unconscious once per Long Rest
- Turns a killing blow into 1 HP
- Gives you another turn to heal, flee, or finish the fight
- Works against any damage that doesn't kill outright

## Roleplaying an Orc

### Physical Appearance
- Tall and powerfully built
- Green, gray, or brown skin (varies by world)
- Prominent lower tusks
- Often have scarification or tribal markings

### Personality Considerations
- May value strength, honor, or tribal loyalty
- Could be working to overcome orcish stereotypes
- Might embrace or reject traditional orcish culture
- Often straightforward and action-oriented

### Background Ideas
- Warrior from a proud orcish nation
- Mercenary seeking fortune
- Exile trying to find a place in the world
- Peacemaker bridging orcish and other cultures

### Cultural Notes
- 2024 rules present orcs neutrally
- No inherent evil alignment
- Culture varies by campaign setting
- Can be from tribal, urban, or mixed backgrounds

## Combat Tips

- **Adrenaline Rush** early in combat to close distance and absorb initial hits
- **Relentless Endurance** lets you take calculated risksâ€”you have a backup
- Use **120 ft. Darkvision** to spot enemies before they see you
- Save at least one Adrenaline Rush for emergency escape
- The temp HP from Adrenaline Rush stacks with other temp HP replacement (takes higher)

## Build Strategies

### Aggressive Melee
- Use Adrenaline Rush turn 1 to engage
- Relentless Endurance keeps you fighting
- High CON for HP + temp HP buffer
- Works with Barbarian, Fighter, Paladin

### Mobile Skirmisher
- Hit and run with Adrenaline Rush
- 120 ft. Darkvision for night raids
- Works with Rogue, Ranger, Monk

### Tactical Support
- Dash to reach downed allies
- Survive with Relentless to heal the party
- Works with Cleric, Bard, Druid
