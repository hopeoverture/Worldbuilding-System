# Bard

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Bards are versatile spellcasters who use music and performance to weave magic, inspire allies, and manipulate the battlefield.

---

## Class Basics

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Primary Ability** | Charisma |
| **Saving Throws** | Dexterity, Charisma |
| **Armor Proficiencies** | Light |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple |
| **Spellcasting** | Full caster (Arcane) |

### Skills (Choose 3 from any)
Bards can choose any three skills.

## Key Features

### Bardic Inspiration
**Bonus Action**: Give an ally within 60 feet an Inspiration die. Within 10 minutes, they can roll it and add to one:
- Ability check
- Attack roll
- Saving throw

Die size increases: d6 → d8 (5th) → d10 (10th) → d12 (15th)
Uses equal to **Charisma modifier** (minimum 1), regain on **Long Rest**.

### Font of Inspiration (5th Level)
Regain all Bardic Inspiration on a **Short Rest**.

### Jack of All Trades
Add half your Proficiency Bonus (rounded down) to any ability check that doesn't already include your Proficiency Bonus.

### Expertise
Choose 2 skills: double your Proficiency Bonus for those skills. Choose 2 more at 9th level.

### Countercharm (6th Level)
As an action, you can perform until the end of your next turn. While performing, you and allies within 30 feet have **Advantage** on saves against Frightened and Charmed.

### Magical Secrets (10th Level)
Learn 2 spells from **any class's spell list**. They count as Bard spells.

### Superior Inspiration (18th Level)
If you have no Bardic Inspiration dice when you roll Initiative, you regain 1.

### Words of Creation (20th Level)
When you give Bardic Inspiration, you can also grant one effect:
- Temporary HP equal to roll
- Speed increased by roll × 5 ft.

## Subclasses

### College of Dance
*Express magic through movement and acrobatics.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Dazzling Footwork**: Unarmored Defense (DEX + CHA), extra movement |
| 6th | **Inspiring Movement**: Reaction to help ally disengage |
| 14th | **Tandem Footwork**: Grant movement to allies |

### College of Glamour
*Fey-touched magic of beauty and command.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Mantle of Inspiration**: Temp HP and movement to allies |
| 6th | **Enthralling Performance**: Charm audience after 1-minute performance |
| 14th | **Unbreakable Majesty**: Attackers must save or attack someone else |

### College of Lore
*Knowledge seekers who collect magical secrets.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Bonus Proficiencies**: 3 more skills; **Cutting Words**: Reduce enemy rolls |
| 6th | **Additional Magical Secrets**: Learn 2 spells early |
| 14th | **Peerless Skill**: Add Inspiration to own checks |

### College of Valor
*Warrior-poets who inspire through martial prowess.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Combat Inspiration**: Inspiration adds to damage/AC; **Martial Training**: Medium armor, shields, martial weapons |
| 6th | **Extra Attack**: Attack twice |
| 14th | **Battle Magic**: Cast spell + attack in same turn |

## Spellcasting

### Spell List
Bards use the **Arcane** spell list with focus on:
- Enchantment (charms, buffs)
- Illusion (deception, concealment)
- Healing (limited but effective)
- Utility (wide variety)

### Key Spells by Level
| Level | Notable Spells |
|-------|---------------|
| 1st | Healing Word, Dissonant Whispers, Faerie Fire |
| 2nd | Hold Person, Suggestion, Heat Metal |
| 3rd | Hypnotic Pattern, Dispel Magic |
| 4th | Greater Invisibility, Polymorph |
| 5th | Synaptic Static, Hold Monster |

### Magical Secrets Recommendations
- **Counterspell**: Essential spell disruption
- **Find Greater Steed**: Mounted combat
- **Wish**: Ultimate 9th-level flexibility
- **Healing Spirit**: Efficient healing (if DM allows)

## Building a Bard

### Ability Priority
1. **Charisma**: Spells and Inspiration
2. **Dexterity**: AC, saves
3. **Constitution**: HP, concentration
4. **Wisdom**: Common saves
5. **Strength/Intelligence**: Dump

### Expertise Recommendations
- **Persuasion**: Face of the party
- **Perception**: Spot threats
- **Stealth**: Infiltration
- **Deception**: Social manipulation

### College Comparisons
| College | Playstyle |
|---------|-----------|
| Dance | Mobile, unarmored, acrobatic |
| Glamour | Buff-focused, fey thematics |
| Lore | Skill monkey, extra spells, debuff |
| Valor | Martial hybrid, medium armor |

### Race Synergies
- **Human**: Extra feat for CHA boost or War Caster
- **Tiefling**: CHA bonus, fire resistance
- **Halfling**: Lucky rerolls on crucial spells
