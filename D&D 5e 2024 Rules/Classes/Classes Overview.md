# Classes Overview

> [!info] Quick Reference
> The 2024 Player's Handbook includes 12 classes, each with 4 subclasses. Your class determines your primary abilities, combat style, and role in the party.

## Class Categories

### Martial Classes
Focus on weapon attacks and physical combat:
- **Barbarian**: Rage-fueled berserker
- **Fighter**: Tactical weapon master
- **Monk**: Unarmed martial artist
- **Rogue**: Sneaky precision striker

### Full Casters
Access to 9th-level spells, spell slots up to 9th level:
- **Bard**: Charisma, Arcane (support/utility)
- **Cleric**: Wisdom, Divine (healing/support)
- **Druid**: Wisdom, Primal (nature/shapeshifting)
- **Sorcerer**: Charisma, Arcane (blasting/metamagic)
- **Wizard**: Intelligence, Arcane (versatile/prepared)

### Half Casters
Access up to 5th-level spells, blend martial and magic:
- **Paladin**: Charisma, Divine (smites/auras)
- **Ranger**: Wisdom, Primal (nature/hunting)

### Pact Magic
Unique casting system:
- **Warlock**: Limited slots that recharge on Short Rest

## Key 2024 Changes

### All Classes
- Subclass features now at consistent levels within each class
- Many features clarified or rebalanced
- Capstones at 19th level improved

### Martial Classes
- **Weapon Mastery** system adds tactical depth
- More consistent Extra Attack progression
- Many ribbon abilities improved

### Spellcasting Classes
- Spell lists reorganized (Arcane, Divine, Primal)
- Concentration rules clarified
- Some spells rebalanced

## Choosing a Class

### Questions to Consider
1. Do you want to cast spells? (Full caster, half caster, or none)
2. Frontline or backline? (Melee tank vs. ranged support)
3. Simple or complex mechanics? (Champion Fighter vs. Wizard)
4. Support others or deal damage? (Cleric vs. Rogue)

### Party Role Considerations
- **Damage**: Barbarian, Fighter, Rogue, Ranger, Warlock
- **Tank**: Barbarian, Fighter, Paladin
- **Healing**: Cleric, Druid, Bard, Paladin
- **Control**: Wizard, Druid, Bard
- **Skills**: Rogue, Bard, Ranger
- **Face**: Bard, Paladin, Warlock, Sorcerer
