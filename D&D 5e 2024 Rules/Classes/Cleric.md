# Cleric

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Clerics are divine spellcasters who draw power from their deity. They're the primary healers but also capable combatants depending on their domain.

## Key Features

### Spellcasting (Prepared)
- Prepare spells from entire Cleric list each day
- Number prepared = WIS modifier + Cleric level
- Domain spells are always prepared and don't count against limit

### Channel Divinity
Uses: 1 (2 at 6th, 3 at 17th). Regain on **Short or Long Rest**.

**Divine Spark** (All Clerics): As an action:
- **Heal**: One creature within 30 ft. regains 1d8 × half Cleric level HP
- **Damage**: One creature makes CON save or takes same amount Radiant damage

### Sear Undead (5th Level)
When you use Turn Undead, turned undead take Radiant damage equal to your WIS modifier + half your Cleric level.

### Blessed Strikes (7th Level)
Choose one (can change at level up):
- **Divine Strike**: +1d8 damage once per turn with weapon
- **Potent Spellcasting**: Add WIS modifier to cantrip damage

### Divine Intervention (10th Level)
**Action**: Request divine aid. Roll percentile dice; if roll ≤ your Cleric level, DM chooses the effect of any Cleric spell or domain spell. If it works, can't use again for 2d4 days.

### Greater Divine Intervention (20th Level)
Divine Intervention always succeeds. Choose the spell effect yourself.

## Subclasses (Domains)

### Life Domain
*Champions of healing and vitality.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Disciple of Life**: Bonus healing; **Life Domain Spells**: Bless, Cure Wounds, etc. |
| 2nd | **Preserve Life**: Channel Divinity mass healing |
| 6th | **Blessed Healer**: Heal yourself when healing others |
| 17th | **Supreme Healing**: Maximize healing dice |

**Domain Spells**: Bless, Cure Wounds, Lesser Restoration, Spiritual Weapon, Revivify, etc.

### Light Domain
*Wielders of radiant fire and revelation.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Warding Flare**: Impose disadvantage on attacker; **Light Domain Spells** |
| 2nd | **Radiance of the Dawn**: Channel Divinity AoE damage |
| 6th | **Improved Warding Flare**: Use Flare on allies |
| 17th | **Corona of Light**: Enemies have disadvantage on saves vs. fire/radiant spells |

**Domain Spells**: Burning Hands, Faerie Fire, Scorching Ray, Fireball, Wall of Fire, etc.

### Trickery Domain
*Deception, illusion, and mischief.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Blessing of the Trickster**: Grant stealth advantage; **Trickery Domain Spells** |
| 2nd | **Invoke Duplicity**: Channel Divinity illusory duplicate |
| 6th | **Trickster's Transposition**: Swap places with duplicate |
| 17th | **Improved Duplicity**: Four duplicates |

**Domain Spells**: Charm Person, Disguise Self, Mirror Image, Pass without Trace, etc.

### War Domain
*Holy warriors and battle commanders.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **War Priest**: Bonus Action attacks; **War Domain Spells**; Martial Weapon + Heavy Armor proficiency |
| 2nd | **Guided Strike**: Channel Divinity +10 to attack roll |
| 6th | **War God's Blessing**: Grant Guided Strike to ally |
| 17th | **Avatar of Battle**: Resistance to weapon damage |

**Domain Spells**: Divine Favor, Shield of Faith, Magic Weapon, Spirit Guardians, etc.

### Spell Preparation
Unlike "spells known" casters, Clerics can prepare different spells each day. This gives incredible flexibility—prepare utility spells for exploration, combat spells for dungeons.

## Building a Cleric

### Ability Priority
1. **Wisdom**: Spells, Channel Divinity
2. **Constitution**: HP, concentration
3. **Strength or Dexterity**: Depending on armor choice
4. **Charisma**: Social, some saves
5. **Intelligence**: Dump stat

### Domain Comparison
| Domain | Role | Armor |
|--------|------|-------|
| Life | Dedicated healer | Medium |
| Light | Blaster, AoE | Medium |
| Trickery | Utility, infiltration | Medium |
| War | Frontline combatant | Heavy |

### Race Synergies
- **Dwarf**: CON bonus, extra HP, 120 ft. Darkvision
- **Human**: Extra feat (War Caster)
- **Aasimar**: Healing, radiant damage theme
