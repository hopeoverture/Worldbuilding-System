# Druid

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Druids are primal spellcasters who draw power from nature. They can shapeshift into animals and command the forces of the natural world.

---

| **Primary Ability** | Wisdom |
| **Saving Throws** | Intelligence, Wisdom |
| **Armor Proficiencies** | Light, Medium, Shields (non-metal) |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple |
| **Spellcasting** | Full caster (Primal), prepared |

## Key Features

### Druidic
Secret language of druids. Leave hidden messages others can't decipher without magic.

### Primal Order (1st Level)
Choose one:
- **Magician**: Learn one Druid cantrip (doesn't count against limit)
- **Warden**: Proficiency with Martial weapons and Medium Armor training

### Wild Shape (2nd Level)
**Bonus Action** to transform into a beast form. Initially:
- Max CR = 1/4 (no flying speed)
- Can't cast spells but concentration maintained
- Temp HP equal to beast's HP
- Use beast's physical stats, keep mental stats
- Lasts hours = half Druid level (minimum 1)

Uses: 2 (increases at higher levels), regain on **Short or Long Rest**.

### Wild Companion (2nd Level)
Expend Wild Shape use to cast *Find Familiar* without material components.

### Wild Resurgence (5th Level)
If you have no Wild Shape uses, you can expend a spell slot to regain one use.

### Elemental Fury (7th Level)
Choose one cantrip damage type: Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder. Once per turn, add that damage to one attack.

### Beast Spells (18th Level)
Cast spells while in Wild Shape.

### Archdruid (20th Level)
Unlimited Wild Shape uses. Can ignore verbal, somatic, and material components (without cost).

## Subclasses (Circles)

### Circle of the Land
*Mystics who draw power from specific terrains.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Land's Aid**: Heal with Wild Shape; **Circle Spells**: Choose terrain, gain always-prepared spells |
| 6th | **Natural Recovery**: Regain spell slots during Short Rest |
| 10th | **Nature's Ward**: Immune to poison, disease; can't be charmed/frightened by elementals/fey |
| 14th | **Nature's Sanctuary**: Beasts and plants must save to attack you |

**Terrain Options**: Arctic, Coast, Desert, Forest, Grassland, Mountain, Swamp, Underdark

### Circle of the Moon
*Fierce warriors who master combat Wild Shapes.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Combat Wild Shape**: Bonus Action to shift, higher CR beasts (CR 1) |
| 6th | **Circle Forms**: Wild Shape into CR = Druid level ÷ 3; attacks count as magical |
| 10th | **Elemental Wild Shape**: Transform into Air, Earth, Fire, or Water elemental |
| 14th | **Thousand Forms**: Cast *Alter Self* at will |

### Circle of the Sea
*Druids connected to ocean and storm.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Wrath of the Sea**: Bonus Action push/knock prone; **Circle Spells** |
| 6th | **Aquatic Affinity**: Swim speed, water breathing |
| 10th | **Stormborn**: Fly speed in rain, resistance to lightning/thunder |
| 14th | **Oceanic Gift**: Grant allies swim speed and water breathing |

### Circle of the Stars
*Stargazers who channel celestial magic.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Star Map**: Free *Guidance*, extra spells; **Starry Form**: Wild Shape for constellations instead |
| 6th | **Cosmic Omen**: Reactions to boost or hinder d20 rolls |
| 10th | **Twinkling Constellations**: Change Starry Form, additional benefits |
| 14th | **Full of Stars**: Resistance to B/P/S while in Starry Form |

**Starry Forms** (Archer, Chalice, Dragon): Each provides different combat benefits.

## Building a Druid

### Ability Priority
1. **Wisdom**: Spells and DCs
2. **Constitution**: HP, concentration
3. **Dexterity**: AC (unless Warden)
4. **Strength**: For Warden builds
5. **Intelligence/Charisma**: Dump

### Circle Comparison
| Circle | Playstyle |
|--------|-----------|
| Land | Versatile caster, terrain specialist |
| Moon | Combat shapeshifter, melee tank |
| Sea | Water/storm themed, control |
| Stars | Ranged support, consistent damage |

### Key Spells
| Level | Must-Have Spells |
|-------|-----------------|
| 1st | Healing Word, Entangle, Goodberry |
| 2nd | Spike Growth, Pass without Trace, Moonbeam |
| 3rd | Conjure Animals, Call Lightning |
| 4th | Polymorph, Conjure Woodland Beings |
| 5th | Conjure Elemental, Wall of Stone |

### Race Synergies
- **Wood Elf**: Extra speed, stealth, WIS-based spells
- **Gnome**: Mental save advantage, size for Wild Shape
- **Human**: Extra feat (War Caster, Sentinel)
