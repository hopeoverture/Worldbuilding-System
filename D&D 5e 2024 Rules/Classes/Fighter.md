# Fighter

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Fighters are supreme warriors, masters of weapons and combat techniques. They gain the most attacks and have access to all weapons and armor.

---

## Class Basics

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Hit Die** | d10 |
| **Primary Ability** | Strength or Dexterity |
| **Saving Throws** | Strength, Constitution |
| **Armor Proficiencies** | Light, Medium, Heavy, Shields |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple, Martial |
| **Weapon Mastery** | 3 weapons at 1st level |

### Skills (Choose 2)
Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, History, Insight, Intimidation, Perception, Survival

## Key Features

### Fighting Style (1st Level)
Choose one (can change when gaining a Fighter level):
- **Archery**: +2 to ranged weapon attacks
- **Blind Fighting**: 10 ft. Blindsight
- **Defense**: +1 AC while wearing armor
- **Dueling**: +2 damage with one-handed weapon
- **Great Weapon Fighting**: Reroll 1s and 2s on damage
- **Interception**: Reduce damage to nearby ally (Reaction)
- **Protection**: Impose disadvantage on attack against nearby ally (Reaction)
- **Thrown Weapon Fighting**: +2 damage, draw as part of attack
- **Two-Weapon Fighting**: Add ability modifier to off-hand damage
- **Unarmed Fighting**: d6/d8 unarmed damage, grapple damage

### Second Wind
**Bonus Action**: Heal 1d10 + Fighter level. Uses = Proficiency Bonus per Long Rest.

### Action Surge (2nd Level)
Take an additional Action. One use per Short/Long Rest (two at 17th).

### Tactical Mind (2nd Level)
Add 1d10 to a failed ability check. If still fails, use isn't expended.

### Extra Attack
Attack multiple times when taking the Attack action:
- **5th level**: 2 attacks
- **11th level**: 3 attacks
- **20th level**: 4 attacks

### Tactical Shift (5th Level)
When you use Second Wind, move up to half your speed without provoking.

### Indomitable (9th Level)
Reroll a failed saving throw (must use new roll). Uses increase at 13th and 17th.

### Master of Armaments (9th Level)
Change one Weapon Mastery option after Long Rest.

### Studied Attacks (13th Level)
If you miss a creature twice, your next attack against it has Advantage.

## Subclasses

### Battle Master
*Tactical superiority through combat maneuvers.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Combat Superiority**: 4 Superiority Dice (d8), 3 maneuvers; **Student of War**: Tool proficiency |
| 7th | **Know Your Enemy**: Learn creature's capabilities |
| 10th | **Improved Combat Superiority**: d10 Superiority Dice, 2 more maneuvers |
| 15th | **Relentless**: Regain 1 die when you have none at initiative |
| 18th | **Ultimate Combat Superiority**: d12 Superiority Dice |

**Maneuvers** (Examples): Disarming Attack, Goading Attack, Menacing Attack, Precision Attack, Riposte, Trip Attack

### Champion
*Pure martial excellence through critical hits and athletics.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Improved Critical**: Crit on 19-20; **Remarkable Athlete**: Add half PB to STR/DEX/CON checks |
| 7th | **Additional Fighting Style** |
| 10th | **Heroic Warrior**: Gain Heroic Inspiration if you have none at start of turn |
| 15th | **Superior Critical**: Crit on 18-20 |
| 18th | **Survivor**: Regenerate HP when below half max |

### Eldritch Knight
*Combines martial prowess with arcane magic.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Spellcasting**: INT-based Wizard spells; **War Bond**: Summon bonded weapons |
| 7th | **War Magic**: Cast cantrip + Bonus Action attack |
| 10th | **Eldritch Strike**: Disadvantage on saves vs. your spells after hit |
| 15th | **Arcane Charge**: Teleport when Action Surging |
| 18th | **Improved War Magic**: Cast spell + Bonus Action attack |

### Psi Warrior
*Psionic powers fuel telekinetic combat abilities.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Psionic Power**: Psionic dice for Protective Field, Psionic Strike, Telekinetic Movement |
| 7th | **Telekinetic Adept**: Psi-Powered Leap, Telekinetic Thrust |
| 10th | **Guarded Mind**: Resistance to psychic, clear charm/frighten |
| 15th | **Bulwark of Force**: Create force field for half cover |
| 18th | **Telekinetic Master**: Cast *Telekinesis* |

## Building a Fighter

### Ability Priority (Strength)
1. **Strength**: Attack and damage
2. **Constitution**: HP, saves
3. **Dexterity**: Initiative, Finesse backup
4. **Wisdom**: Common saves
5. **Intelligence**: EK spells / dump
6. **Charisma**: Dump

### Ability Priority (Dexterity)
1. **Dexterity**: Attack, AC, saves
2. **Constitution**: HP
3. **Wisdom**: Common saves
4. **Strength/Intelligence/Charisma**: Dump

### Subclass Comparison
| Subclass | Complexity | Playstyle |
|----------|------------|-----------|
| Battle Master | Medium | Tactical maneuvers |
| Champion | Low | Reliable crits, simple |
| Eldritch Knight | High | Spells + martial |
| Psi Warrior | Medium | Telekinetic tricks |

### Race Synergies
- **Human**: Extra feat (Great Weapon Master, Polearm Master)
- **Goliath**: Stone's Endurance, damage reduction
- **Dwarf**: Extra HP, poison resistance
