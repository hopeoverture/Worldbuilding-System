# Paladin

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Paladins are holy warriors who blend martial prowess with divine magic. They smite foes with radiant damage and protect allies with auras.

## Key Features

### Lay on Hands
Pool of healing equal to **5 × Paladin level**. As a **Bonus Action**:
- Touch a creature and restore HP from pool
- Spend 5 points to remove Poisoned condition
- Can't use on Undead or Constructs

### Divine Smite (2nd Level)
When you hit with a melee attack, expend a spell slot to deal extra Radiant damage:
- **1st level slot**: 2d8
- **Higher slots**: +1d8 per level above 1st (max 5d8)
- **+1d8** against Undead or Fiends

### Fighting Style (2nd Level)
Choose one (can change when gaining Paladin level):
- Defense, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Protection

### Channel Divinity (3rd Level)
One use per Short/Long Rest. Each Oath grants unique options.

**Divine Sense** (All Paladins): As an action, know location of any Celestial, Fiend, or Undead within 60 ft.

### Extra Attack (5th Level)
Attack twice when taking Attack action.

### Faithful Steed (5th Level)
Can cast *Find Steed* without a spell slot once per Long Rest.

### Aura of Protection (6th Level)
You and friendly creatures within 10 feet add your **Charisma modifier** to all saving throws.

### Aura of Courage (10th Level)
You and friendly creatures within 10 feet can't be Frightened.

### Radiant Strikes (11th Level)
Your melee attacks deal an extra **1d8 Radiant** damage.

### Restoring Touch (14th Level)
Can use Lay on Hands to remove:
- Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, Stunned
- Cost: 5 HP from pool per condition

### Aura Expansion (18th Level)
Auras extend to **30 feet**.

## Building a Paladin

### Ability Priority
1. **Strength**: Attack and damage
2. **Charisma**: Spells, Aura, saves
3. **Constitution**: HP
4. **Wisdom**: Some saves
5. **Dexterity/Intelligence**: Dump

### Divine Smite Strategy
- **Save slots** for critical hits (double smite dice!)
- **Higher slots** for bosses
- **Low slots** for finishing blows
- Balance smiting vs. spell utility

### Weapon Mastery Choices
- **Longsword** (Sap): Disadvantage on enemy's next attack
- **Greatsword** (Graze): Damage even on miss
- **Halberd** (Cleave): Hit multiple enemies

### Race Synergies
- **Human**: Extra feat (Great Weapon Master, Polearm Master)
- **Aasimar**: Radiant theme, CHA bonus
- **Dragonborn**: Fire Breath adds AoE, STR-based

### Multiclass Options
- **Sorcerer** (2-3): Metamagic for Quickened Hold Person → Smite
- **Warlock** (2): Short rest slots for more smites
- **Bard** (2-3): More CHA synergy, Expertise
