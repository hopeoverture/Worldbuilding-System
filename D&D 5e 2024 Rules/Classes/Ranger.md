# Ranger

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Rangers are skilled hunters and trackers who combine martial abilities with primal magic. They excel in wilderness exploration and against specific prey.

## Key Features

### Favored Enemy (1st Level)
You always have *Hunter's Mark* prepared. You can cast it **twice without a spell slot**, regaining uses on Long Rest. Concentration is easier (DC 10).

### Deft Explorer (2nd Level)
Choose two of:
- **Canny**: Expertise in one skill; two extra languages
- **Roamer**: Climbing and Swimming speeds equal walking speed
- **Tireless**: Temp HP on Short Rest; reduce Exhaustion on Short Rest

### Fighting Style (2nd Level)
Choose one:
- **Archery**: +2 to ranged attacks
- **Defense**: +1 AC
- **Dueling**: +2 damage one-handed
- **Two-Weapon Fighting**: Add modifier to off-hand

### Extra Attack (5th Level)
Attack twice when taking Attack action.

### Roving (6th Level)
Speed increases by **10 feet** while not wearing heavy armor.

### Expertise (9th Level)
Double proficiency in two skills.

### Tireless (10th Level)
Recover temp HP (1d8 + WIS) equal to PB times per Long Rest. Short Rest reduces Exhaustion by 1.

### Relentless Hunter (13th Level)
Your Favored Enemy feature grants extra Hunter's Mark uses equal to WIS modifier.

### Nature's Veil (14th Level)
**Bonus Action** to become Invisible until start of next turn. Uses = WIS modifier per Long Rest.

### Feral Senses (18th Level)
Can't have Disadvantage on attacks against creatures you can hear but not see. Know location of Invisible creatures within 30 feet.

### Foe Slayer (20th Level)
Add WIS modifier to attack OR damage rolls against Favored Enemy-marked targets.

## Subclasses (Conclaves)

### Beast Master
*Fight alongside a loyal animal companion.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Primal Companion**: Beast of the Land, Sea, or Sky companion |
| 7th | **Exceptional Training**: Companion Dash, Disengage, Help as bonus action |
| 11th | **Bestial Fury**: Companion attacks twice |
| 15th | **Share Spells**: Buff spells affect companion too |

**Companion Types**:
- **Beast of the Land**: Melee attacks, Charge
- **Beast of the Sea**: Swim speed, Binding Strike
- **Beast of the Sky**: Fly speed, Flyby

### Fey Wanderer
*Touched by the Feywild with fey magic.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Dreadful Strikes**: Psychic damage on attacks; **Otherworldly Glamour**: CHA checks use WIS; **Fey Wanderer Spells** |
| 7th | **Beguiling Twist**: Redirect charms and fears |
| 11th | **Fey Reinforcements**: Summon fey creatures |
| 15th | **Misty Wanderer**: Cast *Misty Step* free, bring ally |

### Gloom Stalker
*Shadow hunter who strikes from darkness.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Dread Ambusher**: Bonus speed and attack first turn; **Umbral Sight**: Darkvision, invisible to Darkvision; **Gloom Stalker Spells** |
| 7th | **Iron Mind**: WIS save proficiency (or INT if already have WIS) |
| 11th | **Stalker's Flurry**: When you miss, make another attack |
| 15th | **Shadowy Dodge**: Reaction to impose disadvantage on attack |

### Hunter
*Traditional monster slayer with combat techniques.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Hunter's Prey**: Choose Colossus Slayer, Horde Breaker, or Giant Killer |
| 7th | **Defensive Tactics**: Escape the Horde, Steel Will, or Multiattack Defense |
| 11th | **Superior Hunter's Defense**: Evasion, Stand Against the Tide, or Uncanny Dodge |
| 15th | **Multiattack**: Volley or Whirlwind Attack |

| 5th | Swift Quiver, Conjure Volley |

### Hunter's Mark
Now a class feature! Key points:
- **Free casts**: WIS modifier times per Long Rest
- **Damage**: +1d6 to attacks against marked target
- **Tracking**: Advantage on Survival/Perception to find target
- **Concentration**: Easier to maintain (DC 10)

## Building a Ranger

### Ability Priority
1. **Dexterity**: Attacks, AC, initiative
2. **Wisdom**: Spells, class features
3. **Constitution**: HP
4. **Intelligence/Strength/Charisma**: Dump

### Subclass Comparison
| Subclass | Role |
|----------|------|
| Beast Master | Pet class, action economy |
| Fey Wanderer | Social ranger, psychic damage |
| Gloom Stalker | Ambusher, darkness specialist |
| Hunter | Versatile damage, simple |

### Weapon Mastery Choices
- **Longbow** (Slow): Reduce speed for kiting
- **Shortsword** (Vex): Advantage setup for TWF
- **Rapier** (Vex): Dueling builds

### Race Synergies
- **Wood Elf**: Speed, Perception, stealth
- **Human**: Extra feat (Sharpshooter, Sentinel)
- **Halfling**: Lucky, stealth, hiding
