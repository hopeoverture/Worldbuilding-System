# Rogue

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Rogues are skill experts and precision strikers who deal massive damage through Sneak Attack. They excel at stealth, locks, traps, and getting in and out of danger.

---

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Primary Ability** | Dexterity |
| **Saving Throws** | Dexterity, Intelligence |
| **Armor Proficiencies** | Light |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple, Martial (Finesse or Light) |
| **Weapon Mastery** | 2 weapons at 1st level |

### Skills (Choose 4)
Acrobatics, Athletics, Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Investigation, Perception, Performance, Persuasion, Sleight of Hand, Stealth

## Key Features

### Sneak Attack
Once per turn, deal extra damage when:
- Attack with Finesse or Ranged weapon, AND
- Have Advantage on the attack, OR
- Enemy of target is within 5 ft. of target and you don't have Disadvantage

Damage: Scales from 1d6 to 10d6 (see table).

### Expertise
Double proficiency in chosen skills/tools. 2 at 1st level, 2 more at 6th.

### Thieves' Cant
Secret code language of rogues. Takes 4× longer than normal speech.

### Cunning Action (2nd Level)
**Bonus Action**: Dash, Disengage, or Hide.

### Steady Aim (3rd Level)
**Bonus Action**: Gain Advantage on next attack if you don't move this turn.

### Cunning Strike (5th Level)
When Sneak Attack hits, sacrifice Sneak Attack dice for effects:
- **Disarm** (1d6): Target drops one item
- **Poison** (1d6): Target makes CON save or Poisoned
- **Trip** (1d6): Target makes DEX save or falls Prone
- **Withdraw** (1d6): Move half speed without provoking

### Uncanny Dodge (5th Level)
**Reaction** when hit: Halve the attack's damage.

### Evasion (7th Level)
DEX saves for half damage → take none on success, half on fail.

### Reliable Talent (7th Level)
Treat any d20 roll of 9 or lower as 10 for proficient ability checks.

### Improved Cunning Strike (11th Level)
Additional options:
- **Daze** (2d6): Target makes CON save or can't take Reactions
- **Knock Out** (6d6): Target makes CON save or Unconscious for 1 minute
- **Obscure** (3d6): Impose Blindness for next turn

### Devious Strikes (14th Level)
Additional options:
- **Knock Out** reduced to 5d6
- **Obscure** reduced to 2d6

### Slippery Mind (15th Level)
Proficiency in Wisdom saving throws.

### Elusive (18th Level)
No attack roll has Advantage against you unless Incapacitated.

### Stroke of Luck (20th Level)
Miss → Hit, or Failed check → 20. Once per Short Rest.

## Subclasses

### Arcane Trickster
*Roguish magic through Wizard spells.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Spellcasting**: INT-based Wizard spells (Enchant/Illusion focus); **Mage Hand Legerdemain** |
| 9th | **Magical Ambush**: Disadvantage on saves vs. spells from Hidden |
| 13th | **Versatile Trickster**: Mage Hand grants Advantage |
| 17th | **Spell Thief**: Steal spells from targets |

### Assassin
*Master of disguise and lethal ambush.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Assassinate**: Advantage vs. creatures that haven't acted; auto-crit vs. Surprised; **Assassin's Tools** |
| 9th | **Infiltration Expertise**: Create false identities |
| 13th | **Envenom Weapons**: Apply poison as Bonus Action; extra damage |
| 17th | **Death Strike**: Double Assassinate damage (must save) |

### Soulknife
*Psionic rogue with psychic blades.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Psionic Power**: Psi die for skills and communication; **Psychic Blades**: Summon psychic weapons |
| 9th | **Soul Blades**: Teleport to blade, use Psi die for attacks |
| 13th | **Psychic Veil**: Become Invisible |
| 17th | **Rend Mind**: Stun with Sneak Attack |

### Thief
*Acrobatic burglar and treasure hunter.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Fast Hands**: Use object as Bonus Action; **Second-Story Work**: Climb speed, reduced falling damage |
| 9th | **Supreme Sneak**: Advantage on Stealth while moving half speed |
| 13th | **Use Magic Device**: Ignore class/race/level requirements |
| 17th | **Thief's Reflexes**: Two turns first round of combat |

## Building a Rogue

### Ability Priority
1. **Dexterity**: Attacks, AC, skills
2. **Constitution**: HP
3. **Wisdom**: Perception, saves
4. **Intelligence**: Arcane Trickster / Investigation
5. **Strength/Charisma**: Dump or social focus

### Expertise Recommendations
Must-haves:
- **Stealth**: Core Rogue skill
- **Perception**: Spot threats and traps
- **Thieves' Tools**: If dungeon delving

Good choices:
- **Deception/Persuasion**: Face role
- **Acrobatics**: Escape grapples
- **Investigation**: Find hidden things

### Subclass Comparison
| Subclass | Role |
|----------|------|
| Arcane Trickster | Utility spells, mage hand tricks |
| Assassin | Alpha strike, social infiltration |
| Soulknife | Psionic utility, reliable damage |
| Thief | Object use, climbing, item versatility |

### Sneak Attack Sources
- Party members engaged with target
- Flanking (if using optional rule)
- Hidden → Steady Aim
- Familiar (using Help action)
- Advantage from any source

### Race Synergies
- **Halfling**: Lucky rerolls, Naturally Stealthy
- **Elf**: Perception, Trance
- **Human**: Extra feat (Alert, Lucky)
