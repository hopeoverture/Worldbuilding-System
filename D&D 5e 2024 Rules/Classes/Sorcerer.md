# Sorcerer

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Sorcerers are innate spellcasters whose magic comes from within. They use Sorcery Points and Metamagic to modify spells in ways other casters cannot.

---

## Class Basics

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Hit Die** | d6 |
| **Primary Ability** | Charisma |
| **Saving Throws** | Constitution, Charisma |
| **Armor Proficiencies** | None |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple |
| **Spellcasting** | Full caster (Arcane), spells known |

### Skills (Choose 2)
Arcana, Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Persuasion, Religion

## Key Features

### Font of Magic (2nd Level)
**Sorcery Points** = Sorcerer level. Use for:
- **Creating Spell Slots**: Spend points to create slots (see table)
- **Converting Slots**: Convert spell slot to points equal to slot level
- **Metamagic**: Power spell modifications

| Spell Slot | Point Cost |
|------------|------------|
| 1st | 2 |
| 2nd | 3 |
| 3rd | 5 |
| 4th | 6 |
| 5th | 7 |

### Metamagic
Choose 2 options at 2nd level, 1 more at 10th and 17th.

| Metamagic | Cost | Effect |
|-----------|------|--------|
| **Careful** | 1 | Chosen creatures auto-succeed on spell save |
| **Distant** | 1 | Double range; touch becomes 30 ft. |
| **Empowered** | 1 | Reroll damage dice (up to CHA mod), use new |
| **Extended** | 1 | Double duration (max 24 hours) |
| **Heightened** | 3 | One target has disadvantage on first save |
| **Quickened** | 2 | Cast action spell as bonus action |
| **Seeking** | 1 | Reroll missed spell attack |
| **Subtle** | 1 | No verbal or somatic components |
| **Transmuted** | 1 | Change damage type (Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Poison, Thunder) |
| **Twinned** | Slot level | Target second creature with single-target spell |

### Arcane Apotheosis (20th Level)
While no Sorcery Points remain:
- 1st-level spells cost 0 points to Quicken or Subtle
- Regain 4 Sorcery Points when you finish a Short Rest

## Subclasses (Origins)

### Aberrant Sorcery
*Psionic powers from aberrant influence.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Psionic Spells**: Extra spells known; **Telepathic Speech**: Communicate mentally |
| 6th | **Psionic Sorcery**: Cast psionic spells with Sorcery Points, no components |
| 14th | **Revelation in Flesh**: Transform body (see, fly, squeeze, swim) |
| 18th | **Warp Reality**: Short-range teleport allies, deal psychic damage |

### Clockwork Sorcery
*Order magic from Mechanus.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Clockwork Magic**: Extra spells; **Restore Balance**: Negate advantage/disadvantage |
| 6th | **Bastion of Law**: Ward that absorbs damage |
| 14th | **Trance of Order**: Treat rolls below 10 as 10 |
| 18th | **Clockwork Cavalcade**: Heal and repair in area |

### Draconic Sorcery
*Dragon blood grants elemental power.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Draconic Resilience**: +1 HP per level, AC 13+DEX; **Dragon Ancestor**: Choose element |
| 6th | **Elemental Affinity**: Add CHA to damage of your element |
| 14th | **Dragon Wings**: Fly speed |
| 18th | **Draconic Presence**: Frightening or charming aura |

### Wild Magic
*Chaotic magical surges.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Wild Magic Surge**: Roll on surge table; **Tides of Chaos**: Gain advantage, trigger surge |
| 6th | **Bend Luck**: Add/subtract d4 from creature's roll |
| 14th | **Controlled Chaos**: Roll twice on surge table, choose |
| 18th | **Spell Bombardment**: Maximize one damage die |

## Metamagic Deep Dive

### Top Tier
- **Quickened**: Cast *Fireball* then *Fire Bolt* in same turn
- **Twinned**: Double *Haste*, *Polymorph*, *Disintegrate*
- **Subtle**: Undetectable casting, works in Silence

### Situational
- **Heightened**: Excellent for save-or-suck spells
- **Empowered**: Consistent damage boost, cheap
- **Careful**: Protect allies from your AoE

### Niche
- **Extended**: Double *Mage Armor* or buffs
- **Distant**: Tactical flexibility
- **Transmuted**: Bypass resistances

## Building a Sorcerer

### Ability Priority
1. **Charisma**: Spells, DCs, class features
2. **Constitution**: HP, concentration
3. **Dexterity**: AC, initiative
4. **Wisdom**: Common saves
5. **Strength/Intelligence**: Dump

### Subclass Comparison
| Origin | Playstyle |
|--------|-----------|
| Aberrant | Psionic, efficient casting, telepathy |
| Clockwork | Control, reliability, protection |
| Draconic | Blaster, durability, eventual flight |
| Wild Magic | Chaotic, risk/reward, fun |

### Key Spells
| Level | Essential Spells |
|-------|-----------------|
| 1st | Shield, Magic Missile, Sleep |
| 2nd | Misty Step, Web, Hold Person |
| 3rd | Fireball, Counterspell, Haste |
| 4th | Polymorph, Greater Invisibility |
| 5th | Synaptic Static, Hold Monster |

### Twinnable Spells (Best Targets)
- **Haste**: Two martial allies = huge DPR
- **Polymorph**: Two T-Rexes
- **Greater Invisibility**: Two invisible assassins
- **Disintegrate**: Two potential kills

### Race Synergies
- **Tiefling**: CHA, fire resistance
- **Human**: Extra feat (War Caster)
- **Aasimar**: CHA, healing, radiant damage
