# Warlock

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Warlocks derive power from pacts with otherworldly patrons. They have few spell slots that recharge on Short Rests, and customize their abilities through Invocations.

## Key Features

### Pact Magic
Unique spellcasting:
- **Few slots** (max 4), but always cast at highest level
- **Recharge on Short Rest** (not just Long Rest)
- Spell slot level caps at 5th
- **Mystic Arcanum**: 6th-9th level spells (1/Long Rest each)

### Eldritch Invocations (2nd Level)
Customizable abilities. Choose from many options, some require prerequisites.

**Popular Invocations**:
| Invocation | Effect |
|------------|--------|
| Agonizing Blast | Add CHA to *Eldritch Blast* damage |
| Repelling Blast | Push 10 ft. with *Eldritch Blast* |
| Devil's Sight | See in magical darkness |
| Mask of Many Faces | At-will *Disguise Self* |
| Misty Visions | At-will *Silent Image* |
| Eldritch Smite | Expend slot for extra damage + prone |
| Thirsting Blade | Extra Attack with Pact Weapon |

### Magical Cunning (2nd Level)
Once per Long Rest, regain half your slots (rounded up) as an action.

### Pact Boon (3rd Level)
Choose one:
- **Pact of the Blade**: Summon magical weapon; can be your spellcasting focus
- **Pact of the Chain**: *Find Familiar* with special options (Imp, Quasit, Sprite, Pseudodragon)
- **Pact of the Tome**: Book with 3 cantrips from any list

### Contact Patron (9th Level)
Cast *Contact Other Plane* without spell slot once per Long Rest.

### Mystic Arcanum (11th+)
Learn one spell each of 6th, 7th, 8th, and 9th level. Cast each once per Long Rest.

### Eldritch Master (20th Level)
Once per Long Rest, spend 1 minute to regain all Pact Magic slots.

## Subclasses (Patrons)

### Archfey Patron
*Pact with a powerful fey lord.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Fey Presence**: Charm or frighten nearby; **Archfey Spells** |
| 6th | **Misty Escape**: Reaction invisibility + teleport when damaged |
| 10th | **Beguiling Defenses**: Immune to charm, reflect |
| 14th | **Dark Delirium**: Illusory imprisonment |

### Celestial Patron
*Pact with a being of the Upper Planes.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Healing Light**: d6 pool for bonus action healing; **Celestial Spells** |
| 6th | **Radiant Soul**: Resistance to radiant, add CHA to fire/radiant spells |
| 10th | **Celestial Resilience**: Temp HP for party at rest |
| 14th | **Searing Vengeance**: Rise from 0 HP, deal radiant damage |

### Fiend Patron
*Pact with a demon or devil.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Dark One's Blessing**: Temp HP when you reduce hostile to 0; **Fiend Spells** |
| 6th | **Dark One's Own Luck**: Add d10 to ability check or save |
| 10th | **Fiendish Resilience**: Choose damage resistance |
| 14th | **Hurl Through Hell**: Banish + 10d10 psychic |

### Great Old One Patron
*Pact with an alien entity beyond understanding.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 1st | **Awakened Mind**: 30 ft. telepathy; **Great Old One Spells** |
| 6th | **Clairvoyant Combatant**: Initiative advantage, can't be surprised |
| 10th | **Thought Shield**: Resist psychic, reflect damage |
| 14th | **Eldritch Hex**: Curse creature, can teleport nearby |

## Eldritch Blast Build

The signature Warlock damage option:
- **Base**: 1d10 force, 120 ft. range
- **Beams**: 1 at 1st, 2 at 5th, 3 at 11th, 4 at 17th
- **+Agonizing Blast**: +CHA per beam (potentially +20 damage at 17th)
- **+Repelling Blast**: Push 10 ft. per beam (40 ft. total at 17th)

## Building a Warlock

### Ability Priority
1. **Charisma**: Spells, Eldritch Blast, class features
2. **Constitution**: HP, concentration
3. **Dexterity**: AC
4. **Wisdom**: Common saves
5. **Strength/Intelligence**: Dump

### Pact Boon Comparison
| Boon | Best For |
|------|----------|
| Blade | Melee builds (Hexblade-style in future books) |
| Chain | Scouting, Help action, familiars |
| Tome | More cantrips, ritual casting with Invocation |

### Subclass Comparison
| Patron | Role |
|--------|------|
| Archfey | Control, escape, charm |
| Celestial | Healer warlock, radiant damage |
| Fiend | Damage, survivability |
| Great Old One | Psychic, telepathy, stealth |

### Invocation Priorities
Must-have for Blaster:
1. **Agonizing Blast** (damage)
2. **Repelling Blast** or **Eldritch Spear** (control/range)

Utility favorites:
- **Devil's Sight** (see in magical darkness)
- **Mask of Many Faces** (infinite disguise)
- **Misty Visions** (infinite silent image)

### Race Synergies
- **Tiefling**: CHA, fire resistance, thematic
- **Human**: Extra feat (War Caster)
- **Aasimar**: CHA, healing, radiant
