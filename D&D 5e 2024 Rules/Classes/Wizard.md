# Wizard

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Wizards are scholarly spellcasters who learn magic through study. They have the largest spell selection and can prepare different spells each day from their spellbook.

---

| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple |

## Key Features

### Spellcasting (Spellbook)
- **Spellbook**: Contains all spells you know
- **Starting spells**: 6 first-level spells at 1st level
- **Learning spells**: +2 spells per Wizard level
- **Copying spells**: Can copy spells from scrolls/books (time + gold)
- **Preparing spells**: INT modifier + Wizard level from spellbook

### Arcane Recovery (1st Level)
Once per Long Rest, during a Short Rest, regain spell slots with combined level ≤ half Wizard level (rounded up).

### Scholar (2nd Level)
Choose a skill (Arcana, History, Investigation, Nature, or Religion):
- Gain proficiency, or Expertise if already proficient
- You always have one INT-based spell prepared based on skill

| Skill | Always Prepared Spell |
|-------|----------------------|
| Arcana | *Detect Magic* |
| History | *Identify* |
| Investigation | *Detect Poison and Disease* |
| Nature | *Speak with Animals* |
| Religion | *Detect Evil and Good* |

### Memorize Spell (5th Level)
After Long Rest, choose one 1st-level spell from your spellbook. You can cast it without preparing it or using a slot once before your next Long Rest.

### Spell Mastery (18th Level)
Choose one 1st-level and one 2nd-level spell. Cast them at their lowest level without slots.

### Signature Spells (20th Level)
Choose two 3rd-level spells. They're always prepared and you can cast each once at 3rd level without a slot per Short Rest.

## Subclasses (Schools)

### School of Abjuration
*Masters of protective and warding magic.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Abjuration Savant**: Copy abjuration spells faster/cheaper; **Arcane Ward**: Shield that absorbs damage |
| 6th | **Projected Ward**: Extend ward to allies |
| 10th | **Spell Breaker**: Heal with Dispel Magic/Remove Curse |
| 14th | **Spell Resistance**: Advantage on saves vs. spells, resistance to spell damage |

### School of Divination
*Seers who glimpse the future.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Divination Savant**: Copy divination spells faster/cheaper; **Portent**: Roll 2d20 after Long Rest, replace any d20 |
| 6th | **Expert Divination**: Regain slots when casting divination |
| 10th | **Third Eye**: See invisible, ethereal, or read any language |
| 14th | **Greater Portent**: Roll 3d20 for Portent |

### School of Evocation
*Artillery mages specializing in damage.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Evocation Savant**: Copy evocation spells faster/cheaper; **Sculpt Spells**: Allies auto-succeed and take no damage from your AoE |
| 6th | **Potent Cantrip**: Cantrips deal half damage on successful save |
| 10th | **Empowered Evocation**: Add INT to evocation spell damage |
| 14th | **Overchannel**: Maximize damage of 5th-level or lower spell (self-damage after first) |

### School of Illusion
*Masters of deception and trickery.*

| Level | Feature |
|-------|---------|
| 3rd | **Illusion Savant**: Copy illusion spells faster/cheaper; **Improved Minor Illusion**: Sound + image together |
| 6th | **Malleable Illusions**: Change nature of ongoing illusion |
| 10th | **Illusory Self**: Reaction illusion causes attack to miss |
| 14th | **Illusory Reality**: Make one illusion object real for 1 minute |

## Spellbook Management

### Copying Spells
| Source | Time | Cost |
|--------|------|------|
| Another wizard's book | 2 hours/level | 50 GP/level |
| Spell scroll | 2 hours/level | 50 GP/level |

### Spell Preparation
Prepare spells each day: **INT modifier + Wizard level**

Example at 5th level with +4 INT: 9 prepared spells from your spellbook of 14+ spells.

### Must-Copy Spells
Wizards should always seek to copy:
- Ritual spells (cast without preparation)
- Situational utility spells
- Different damage types

## Building a Wizard

### Ability Priority
1. **Intelligence**: Everything runs on INT
2. **Constitution**: HP, concentration
3. **Dexterity**: AC, initiative
4. **Wisdom**: Common saves
5. **Strength/Charisma**: Dump

### Subclass Comparison
| School | Role |
|--------|------|
| Abjuration | Tank wizard, protective |
| Divination | Control, never miss, support |
| Evocation | Blaster, damage dealer |
| Illusion | Utility, creative problem-solving |

### Key Spells by Level
| Level | Must-Have Spells |
|-------|-----------------|
| 1st | Shield, Magic Missile, Sleep, Detect Magic (R), Find Familiar (R) |
| 2nd | Misty Step, Web, Hold Person, Invisibility |
| 3rd | Fireball, Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Fly |
| 4th | Polymorph, Dimension Door, Greater Invisibility |
| 5th | Wall of Force, Synaptic Static, Animate Objects |
| 6th | Contingency, Disintegrate |
| 7th | Forcecage, Simulacrum |
| 8th | Maze, Clone |
| 9th | Wish |

*(R) = Ritual*

### Race Synergies
- **Gnome**: Advantage on INT/WIS/CHA saves vs. magic
- **High Elf**: Extra cantrip, INT spellcasting
- **Human**: Extra feat (War Caster, Alert)
