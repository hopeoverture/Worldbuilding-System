# Rules Glossary

> [!info] Quick Reference
> An alphabetical reference of key game terms and mechanics from the 2024 Player's Handbook.

---

## A

**Ability Check**: A d20 test using an ability to overcome a challenge. Roll d20 + ability modifier + proficiency bonus (if applicable) vs. DC.

**Ability Score**: One of six attributes (STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA) measuring a creature's capabilities. Range typically 1-20 for PCs.

**Action**: One of the main things you do on your turn. See [[Actions]].

**Advantage**: Roll 2d20, use the higher result.

**Armor Class (AC)**: The target number an attack roll must meet or exceed to hit.

**Attack Roll**: A d20 test to hit a target. Roll d20 + ability modifier + proficiency bonus vs. target's AC.

**Attunement**: A magical bond with certain items. Maximum 3 attuned items. Requires Short Rest to attune.

---

## B

**Bloodied**: A creature at half its hit point maximum or fewer. Observable state, not a condition.

**Bonus Action**: A secondary action on your turn, only available if something grants it.

**Burrow Speed**: Speed for moving through earth, sand, mud, or ice.

---

## C

**Cantrip**: A spell of 0th level that doesn't require a spell slot.

**Challenge Rating (CR)**: A monster's threat level. CR equals the level of a party of 4 that would find it a fair challenge.

**Climb Speed**: Speed for moving along vertical surfaces without extra movement cost.

**Concentration**: A requirement of certain spells. Only one concentration spell at a time. Broken by damage (CON save), incapacitation, death, or casting another concentration spell.

**Condition**: A temporary state affecting a creature. See [[Conditions]].

**Cover**: Physical protection granting +2 AC (half), +5 AC (three-quarters), or immunity to targeting (total). See [[Cover and Obscurement]].

**Critical Hit**: Natural 20 on attack roll. Automatic hit, roll damage dice twice.

---

## D

**D20 Test**: The three main dice rolls: ability checks, attack rolls, saving throws. See [[D20 Tests]].

**Damage Resistance**: Take half damage from a damage type.

**Damage Vulnerability**: Take double damage from a damage type.

**Darkvision**: See in dim light as bright light; darkness as dim light (grayscale only).

**Difficult Terrain**: Terrain costing 2 feet of movement per 1 foot traveled.

**Disadvantage**: Roll 2d20, use the lower result.

**Dying**: At 0 HP and making death saving throws.

---

## E

**Exhaustion**: A condition with cumulative levels. Each level: -2 to d20 tests. Level 6+: speed becomes 0. Level 7+: death.

**Experience Points (XP)**: Currency earned through challenges that determines character level.

**Expertise**: Double your proficiency bonus for a specific skill or tool.

---

## F

**Flanking (Optional)**: When two allies are on opposite sides of an enemy, attacks against it have advantage.

**Fly Speed**: Speed for moving through the air.

**Frightened**: Condition causing disadvantage on checks/attacks while source is visible; can't willingly approach source.

---

## G

**Grappled**: Condition causing speed 0. Ends when grappler is incapacitated or target escapes.

**Grappling**: Opposed Strength (Athletics) vs. target's Athletics or Acrobatics to impose Grappled condition.

---

## H

**Heavily Obscured**: Effectively blinded when trying to see into area. Examples: darkness, thick fog.

**Heroic Inspiration**: Allows rerolling any d20 immediately after rolling. Doesn't stack.

**Hidden**: Not perceived by a creature. Attacks against you have disadvantage; your attacks have advantage.

**Hit Dice**: Dice used to determine HP and heal during Short Rests. Type determined by class.

**Hit Points (HP)**: Measure of vitality. At 0: unconscious and dying.

---

## I

**Incapacitated**: Can't take actions, bonus actions, or reactions. Can't concentrate.

**Initiative**: Determines turn order in combat. Roll d20 + DEX modifier.

**Invisible**: Can't be seen without special senses. Attack rolls have advantage; attacks against you have disadvantage.

---

## L

**Lightly Obscured**: Disadvantage on Perception checks to see into area. Examples: dim light, light fog.

**Long Rest**: 8+ hours of rest. Regains all HP, half Hit Dice, all spell slots, removes 1 exhaustion level.

---

## M

**Magic Action**: The action used to cast spells with "1 action" casting time.

**Modifier**: Value derived from ability score: (Score - 10) รท 2, rounded down.

---

## O

**Object**: A discrete, inanimate item. Has AC and HP.

**Opportunity Attack**: Reaction to make one melee attack when hostile creature leaves your reach.

---

## P

**Paralyzed**: Incapacitated, can't move or speak, auto-fail STR/DEX saves, attacks have advantage, melee hits are crits.

**Passive Score**: 10 + all applicable modifiers. Used for repeated tasks or when not actively checking.

**Petrified**: Turned to stone. Incapacitated, resistant to all damage, immune to poison/disease.

**Poisoned**: Disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks.

**Proficiency Bonus**: Bonus added to d20 tests when proficient. Increases with level (+2 to +6).

**Prone**: Movement restricted to crawling. Disadvantage on attacks. Melee attacks against have advantage; ranged have disadvantage.

---

## R

**Reaction**: Special action triggered by specific circumstances. One per round.

**Resistance**: See Damage Resistance.

**Restrained**: Speed 0. Attacks have disadvantage. Attacks against have advantage. DEX saves have disadvantage.

---

## S

**Saving Throw (Save)**: A d20 test to resist an effect. Roll d20 + ability modifier + proficiency bonus (if proficient) vs. DC.

**Short Rest**: 1+ hour of rest. Can spend Hit Dice to heal.

**Size**: Category determining space and carrying capacity. Tiny, Small, Medium, Large, Huge, Gargantuan.

**Speed**: Distance in feet you can move on your turn.

**Spell Attack**: An attack roll using your spellcasting ability.

**Spell DC**: Difficulty Class for saves against your spells. 8 + proficiency + spellcasting modifier.

**Spell Slot**: Resource expended to cast leveled spells.

**Stable**: At 0 HP but no longer making death saves.

**Stunned**: Incapacitated, can't move, speaks falteringly, auto-fail STR/DEX saves, attacks have advantage.

**Swim Speed**: Speed for moving through liquids without extra movement cost.

---

## T

**Temporary Hit Points**: Buffer HP that absorbs damage first. Don't stack. Can't be healed.

**Truesight**: See through magical darkness, invisibility, illusions, and shapechanging. See into Ethereal Plane.

---

## U

**Unarmed Strike**: A melee attack with body parts. 1 + STR modifier bludgeoning damage.

**Unconscious**: Incapacitated, can't move or speak, unaware, drops items, falls prone, auto-fail STR/DEX saves, attacks have advantage, melee hits are crits.

---

## V

**Vulnerability**: See Damage Vulnerability.

---

## W

**Weapon Mastery**: Special properties unlocked by martial classes for specific weapons. See [[Weapons]].
