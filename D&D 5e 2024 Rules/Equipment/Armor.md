# Armor

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Armor provides a base Armor Class (AC). The 2024 rules clarify armor training requirements and remove some penalties.

---

## Light Armor

*Requires Light Armor Training (most classes have this)*

| Armor | AC | Stealth | Cost | Weight |
|-------|-----|---------|------|--------|
| Padded | 11 + DEX | Disadvantage | 5 GP | 8 lb. |
| Leather | 11 + DEX | — | 10 GP | 10 lb. |
| Studded Leather | 12 + DEX | — | 45 GP | 13 lb. |

**Best choice**: Studded Leather (12 + DEX, no penalty)

---

## Medium Armor

*Requires Medium Armor Training*

| Armor | AC | Stealth | Cost | Weight |
|-------|-----|---------|------|--------|
| Hide | 12 + DEX (max 2) | — | 10 GP | 12 lb. |
| Chain Shirt | 13 + DEX (max 2) | — | 50 GP | 20 lb. |
| Scale Mail | 14 + DEX (max 2) | Disadvantage | 50 GP | 45 lb. |
| Breastplate | 14 + DEX (max 2) | — | 400 GP | 20 lb. |
| Half Plate | 15 + DEX (max 2) | Disadvantage | 750 GP | 40 lb. |

**Best choice**: Breastplate (14 + DEX, no penalty) or Half Plate (15 + DEX, stealth penalty)

### Medium Armor Master Feat
- Increases max DEX bonus to +3
- Removes Stealth disadvantage from medium armor

---

## Heavy Armor

*Requires Heavy Armor Training*

| Armor | AC | Stealth | STR | Cost | Weight |
|-------|-----|---------|-----|------|--------|
| Ring Mail | 14 | Disadvantage | — | 30 GP | 40 lb. |
| Chain Mail | 16 | Disadvantage | 13 | 75 GP | 55 lb. |
| Splint | 17 | Disadvantage | 15 | 200 GP | 60 lb. |
| Plate | 18 | Disadvantage | 15 | 1,500 GP | 65 lb. |

**Strength Requirement**: If you don't meet STR requirement, Speed reduced by 10 ft.

**Best choice**: Plate (18 AC) when affordable, Chain Mail (16 AC) early game

---

## Shields

*Requires Shield Training*

| Shield | AC Bonus | Cost | Weight |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| Shield | +2 | 10 GP | 6 lb. |

- Wielded in one hand
- Can't use two-handed weapons while using a shield
- Some features (like Shield Master) require a shield

## Armor Class Calculations

### Standard Calculations

| Situation | AC Formula |
|-----------|-----------|
| Unarmored | 10 + DEX |
| Light Armor | Armor base + DEX |
| Medium Armor | Armor base + DEX (max 2) |
| Heavy Armor | Armor base (no DEX) |
| + Shield | +2 to any above |

### Special AC Calculations

| Source | AC Formula |
|--------|-----------|
| Barbarian Unarmored Defense | 10 + DEX + CON |
| Monk Unarmored Defense | 10 + DEX + WIS |
| Draconic Sorcerer | 13 + DEX |
| *Mage Armor* spell | 13 + DEX |
| Natural Armor (varies) | Usually 13-17 + DEX (some) |

**Note**: Special calculations don't stack with armor. Choose one base calculation.

## Armor Training

### Who Has What

| Training | Classes |
|----------|---------|
| Light | Barbarian, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Monk*, Paladin, Ranger, Rogue, Warlock |
| Medium | Barbarian, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Paladin, Ranger |
| Heavy | Fighter, Paladin |
| Shield | Barbarian, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Paladin, Ranger |

*Monks typically don't wear armor for class features*

### Gaining Training
- **Multiclass**: Gain some proficiencies from new class
- **Feats**: Some feats grant armor training
- **Subclass**: War Cleric, some domains grant heavy armor

## Sleeping in Armor

| Armor | Effect on Long Rest |
|-------|---------------------|
| Light | No penalty |
| Medium | Regain only ¼ of spent Hit Dice (min 1) |
| Heavy | Regain only ¼ of spent Hit Dice (min 1), don't reduce Exhaustion |

## Armor Comparison by AC

| Target AC | Best Options |
|-----------|--------------|
| 12-13 | Studded Leather (high DEX) |
| 14 | Studded Leather (+2 DEX) or Chain Shirt |
| 15 | Studded Leather (+3 DEX) or Breastplate or Half Plate |
| 16 | Chain Mail or Studded Leather (+4 DEX) |
| 17 | Splint or Half Plate (+2 DEX) + Shield |
| 18 | Plate |
| 19 | Half Plate (+2) + Shield or Plate + Ring of Protection |
| 20 | Plate + Shield |

## Cost-Effective Progression

### Light Armor Path
1. Leather (10 GP, AC 11 + DEX)
2. Studded Leather (45 GP, AC 12 + DEX)

### Medium Armor Path
1. Scale Mail (50 GP, AC 14 + DEX max 2)
2. Breastplate (400 GP, AC 14 + DEX max 2, no stealth penalty)
3. Half Plate (750 GP, AC 15 + DEX max 2)

### Heavy Armor Path
1. Chain Mail (75 GP, AC 16)
2. Splint (200 GP, AC 17)
3. Plate (1,500 GP, AC 18)

---

## Stealth Considerations

**No Stealth Penalty**:
- Leather, Studded Leather
- Hide, Chain Shirt, Breastplate

**Stealth Disadvantage**:
- Padded
- Scale Mail, Half Plate
- All Heavy Armor

**Solution**: Medium Armor Master feat removes disadvantage from medium armor.
