## Mounted Combat

### Mounting and Dismounting
- Costs movement equal to **half your speed**
- Must be within 5 feet of mount
- If knocked off, land on feet with DC 10 DEX save, or fall prone

### Controlling a Mount
When you control a mount:
- Mount acts on your initiative
- Mount can only **Dash**, **Disengage**, or **Dodge**
- You direct its movement

### Independent Mounts
Intelligent mounts (or summoned steeds like Find Steed) can act independently:
- Has own initiative
- Can Attack and use any action
- You don't control its actions

### Mounted Combat Benefits
- **Height advantage**: Attack creatures smaller than mount with advantage
- **Mounted Combatant feat**: Redirect attacks to yourself, mount Evasion

### If Your Mount Falls
When mount is knocked prone:
- **Reaction** to dismount and land on feet
- Otherwise, fall prone in space within 5 feet

## Travel Pace

### Overland Travel

| Pace | Per Hour | Per Day | Effect |
|------|----------|---------|--------|
| Fast | 4 miles | 30 miles | -5 passive Perception |
| Normal | 3 miles | 24 miles | — |
| Slow | 2 miles | 18 miles | Can use Stealth |

### Mounted Travel
Mounts can gallop for about 1 hour, covering twice their speed.
- Riding Horse: 120 ft./round for 1 hour = ~12 miles
- After galloping, must walk for 1 hour

### Forced March
After 8 hours of travel, each additional hour:
- DC 10 + hours past 8 Constitution save
- Failure: 1 level of Exhaustion

## Vehicle Proficiency

### Who Has It
- **Sailor Background**: Water vehicles
- **Some backgrounds**: Land vehicles
- **Training**: 250 days, 1 GP/day

### Uses
- Control the vehicle
- Make checks involving the vehicle
- Navigate (with Navigator's Tools)

## Special Mounts

### Find Steed (Paladin)
Summons a spirit mount:
- Warhorse, Pony, Camel, Elk, or Mastiff
- Intelligence 6, understands your languages
- Telepathic bond within 1 mile
- Cast self-targeting spells on both
- Disappears if reduced to 0 HP (can resummon)

### Find Greater Steed
- Griffon, Pegasus, Peryton, Dire Wolf, Rhinoceros, Saber-toothed Tiger
- Flying mounts available

### Phantom Steed
- 100 ft. speed
- Lasts 1 hour
- No combat stats (disappears if damaged)
