# Tools

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Tools allow characters to craft items, perform specialized tasks, and gain unique abilities during downtime or adventure. Proficiency allows you to add your Proficiency Bonus to checks using the tool.

## Other Tools

### Disguise Kit
*Cost: 25 GP | Weight: 3 lb.*

Contains cosmetics, hair dye, small props, and clothing.
- Create disguises
- Advantage on Deception checks while in costume
- Contest against Investigation to see through

### Forgery Kit
*Cost: 15 GP | Weight: 5 lb.*

Contains paper, inks, seals, and reference documents.
- Create fake documents
- Detect forged documents

### Herbalism Kit
*Cost: 5 GP | Weight: 3 lb.*

Contains pouches for herbs, clippers, mortar and pestle.

*Cost: 25 GP | Weight: 2 lb.*

Contains sextant, compass, calipers, ruler, parchment, ink, quill.

### Poisoner's Kit
*Cost: 50 GP | Weight: 2 lb.*

Contains vials, chemicals, mortar and pestle, and safety equipment.
- Create poisons
- Identify poisons
- Apply poison to weapons

### Thieves' Tools
*Cost: 25 GP | Weight: 1 lb.*

Contains lockpicks, small mirror, narrow-bladed scissors, and pliers.
- Open locks (DEX check + proficiency)
- Disarm traps (DEX or INT check + proficiency)
- **Required** for most lock-picking

### Learning Tools
During downtime, you can train to gain tool proficiency:
- Time: 250 days of training
- Cost: 1 GP per day
- Requires a teacher proficient with the tool

## Crafting with Tools

### Basic Crafting Rules
- Need proficiency with relevant tools
- Need raw materials (usually half the item's cost)
- Time: 1 day per 5 GP of item value

### Crafting Magic Items (DMG)
More complex, requires:
- Formula or schematic
- Exotic materials
- Significant time and gold
- Usually DM adjudication
