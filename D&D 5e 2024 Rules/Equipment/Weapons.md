## Weapon Properties

| Property | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **Ammunition** | Requires ammunition; range in parentheses |
| **Finesse** | Use STR or DEX for attack and damage |
| **Heavy** | Small creatures have disadvantage |
| **Light** | Can use for two-weapon fighting |
| **Loading** | One attack per action regardless of attacks |
| **Range** | Normal/Long range in parentheses |
| **Reach** | Melee attacks have 10 ft. reach |
| **Special** | See weapon description |
| **Thrown** | Can throw for ranged attack |
| **Two-Handed** | Requires two hands to use |
| **Versatile** | One or two hands; higher damage in parentheses |

## Weapon Mastery Properties

Classes with Weapon Mastery: **Barbarian**, **Fighter**, **Paladin**, **Ranger**, **Rogue**

| Mastery | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Cleave** | Once per turn on hit, make another attack vs. creature within 5 ft. of first target (within weapon range). Only add modifier if negative. |
| **Graze** | When you miss, deal damage equal to your ability modifier (min 0). No bonus damage applies. |
| **Nick** | When you Attack, you can make the extra attack as part of the Attack action (not Bonus Action). Once per turn. |
| **Push** | On hit vs. Large or smaller creature, push it up to 10 ft. straight away. |
| **Sap** | On hit, target has Disadvantage on its next attack roll before your next turn. |
| **Slow** | On hit and damage, reduce target's Speed by 10 ft. until your next turn. Doesn't stack. |
| **Topple** | On hit, target must succeed on Constitution save (DC = 8 + PB + ability modifier) or fall Prone. |
| **Vex** | On hit, you have Advantage on your next attack roll against that target before end of your next turn. |

---

## Mastery by Weapon Type

### Best for Damage
- **Graze** (Glaive, Greatsword): Never waste an attack
- **Cleave** (Greataxe, Halberd): Hit multiple enemies

### Best for Control
- **Topple** (Quarterstaff, Battleaxe, Maul): Knock prone for advantage
- **Push** (Warhammer, Pike, Heavy Crossbow): Forced movement
- **Slow** (Longbow, Javelin): Kiting enemies

### Best for Action Economy
- **Nick** (Dagger, Scimitar): Extra attack without Bonus Action
- **Vex** (Rapier, Shortsword, Handaxe): Advantage setup

### Best for Debuffing
- **Sap** (Longsword, Mace, Spear): Enemy attack disadvantage
