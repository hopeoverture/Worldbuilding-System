# Epic Boon Feats

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Epic Boon Feats are available only at 19th level, representing the pinnacle of a character's power. Each includes a +1 ability score increase.

---

## All Epic Boons

### Boon of Combat Prowess
*Master of the decisive strike.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 STR or DEX |
| Guaranteed Hit | When you miss, use Reaction to turn miss into a hit |
| Uses | Once per Short/Long Rest |

**Best for**: Martial characters who need that crucial hit to land.

---

### Boon of Dimensional Travel
*Master of spatial movement.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 INT, WIS, or CHA |
| Teleport | Immediately after Attack action, teleport up to 30 ft. (no Opportunity Attacks) |

**Best for**: Skirmishers, hit-and-run tactics, positioning-focused characters.

---

### Boon of Energy Resistance
*Elemental resilience.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 CON |
| Resistance | Gain resistance to two damage types: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Necrotic, Poison, Psychic, Radiant, or Thunder |

**Best for**: Tanks, anyone expecting heavy elemental damage.

---

### Boon of Fate
*Manipulate destiny.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 to any ability |
| Luck Manipulation | When creature within 60 ft. makes d20 test, add or subtract 2d4 (after seeing roll, before result) |
| Uses | Once per Short/Long Rest |

**Best for**: Support characters, clutch save/fail manipulation.

---

### Boon of Fortitude
*Unbreakable vitality.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 CON |
| Hit Point Increase | +40 HP |
| Future HP | +40 more HP at 20th level |

**Best for**: Everyone, especially low-HP classes. Massive durability boost.

---

### Boon of Irresistible Offense
*Unstoppable attacks.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 STR or DEX |
| Bypass Immunity | Your Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage ignores Resistance AND Immunity |

**Best for**: Martial characters fighting enemies with physical damage immunity.

---

### Boon of Recovery
*Regenerative healing.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 CON |
| Regeneration | At start of turn, if you have at least 1 HP and aren't incapacitated, regain 2d10 HP |

**Best for**: Tanks, melee characters, sustained combat.

---

### Boon of Skill
*Legendary competence.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 to any ability |
| Proficiencies | Gain proficiency in all skills |
| Expertise | Gain Expertise in 5 skills of your choice |

**Best for**: Skill monkeys, Bards, Rogues, face characters.

---

### Boon of Speed

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 DEX |
| Speed Increase | +30 ft. to all speeds |

**Best for**: Mobile characters, Monks, chase/escape scenarios.
