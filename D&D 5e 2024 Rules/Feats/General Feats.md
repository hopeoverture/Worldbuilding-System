# General Feats

> [!info] Quick Reference
> General Feats are available at 4th level or higher when you gain an Ability Score Improvement. Most include a +1 ability score increase.

---

## Combat Feats

### Great Weapon Master
*Prerequisite: Proficiency with any Martial weapon*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 STR or DEX |
| Heavy Blows | Before attack, choose -5 attack / +10 damage (Heavy/Versatile weapon) |
| Cleave | On crit or kill, make Bonus Action melee attack |

---

### Polearm Master
*Prerequisite: Proficiency with any Martial weapon*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 STR or DEX |
| Bonus Strike | After Attack action with Glaive/Halberd/Quarterstaff/Spear, Bonus Action 1d4 bludgeoning |
| Reactive Strike | Opportunity Attack when creature enters your reach |

---

### Sentinel
*Prerequisite: Proficiency with any Martial weapon*

| Benefit | Effect |

---

### Sharpshooter
*Prerequisite: Proficiency with any Martial weapon*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 DEX |
| Bypassing Shot | Before attack, choose -5 attack / +10 damage (ranged weapon) |
| No Cover | Ignore half and three-quarters cover |

---

### Shield Master
*Prerequisite: Shield proficiency*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 STR |
| Shield Bash | If you Attack, Bonus Action to shove (push 5 ft. or knock prone) |
| Shield DEX Saves | Add shield's AC bonus to DEX saves vs. single-target effects |
| Evasion-like | If you succeed on DEX save for half damage, take none instead |

---

### Dual Wielder

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 STR or DEX |
| No Light Required | Two-Weapon Fighting works without Light property |
| Draw/Stow | Draw or stow two weapons instead of one |

---

### Crossbow Expert
*Prerequisite: Proficiency with any Martial weapon*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 DEX |
| Ignore Loading | Ignore Loading property of crossbows you're proficient with |
| Close Quarters | No disadvantage for ranged attacks within 5 ft. |
| Rapid Shot | After Attack action with one-handed weapon, Bonus Action hand crossbow attack |

---

### Mounted Combatant

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 STR, DEX, or WIS |
| Advantage | Advantage on attacks vs. unmounted creatures smaller than mount |
| Mount Protection | Redirect attacks from mount to yourself |
| Evasion for Mount | If mount DEX saves for half damage, it takes none (or half on fail) |

## Defensive Feats

### Heavy Armor Master
*Prerequisite: Heavy Armor proficiency*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 STR or CON |
| Damage Reduction | While wearing heavy armor, reduce B/P/S damage by PB |

---

### Medium Armor Master
*Prerequisite: Medium Armor proficiency*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 DEX |
| Better Max DEX | Max DEX bonus to AC in medium armor increases to +3 |
| No Stealth Penalty | Medium armor doesn't impose disadvantage on Stealth |

---

### Resilient

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 to chosen ability |
| Save Proficiency | Gain proficiency in saving throws with that ability |

**Popular choices**: Constitution (concentration), Wisdom (common saves)

---

### Mage Slayer

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 STR or DEX |
| Reactive Strike | When adjacent creature casts a spell, Reaction attack |
| Concentration Breaker | Target has disadvantage on concentration saves from your damage |

---

## Spellcasting Feats

### War Caster
*Prerequisite: Spellcasting or Pact Magic*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 spellcasting ability |
| Concentration | Advantage on Constitution saves to maintain concentration |
| Somatic Freedom | Perform somatic components while hands hold weapons/shield |
| Spell Opportunity | Cast spell instead of attack for Opportunity Attack |

---

### Metamagic Adept
*Prerequisite: Spellcasting or Pact Magic*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Sorcery Points | Gain 2 Sorcery Points (usable only for Metamagic) |
| Metamagic Options | Learn 2 Metamagic options |

---

### Ritual Caster
*Prerequisite: INT, WIS, or CHA 13+*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ritual Book | Gain book with 2 ritual spells from any class |
| Ritual Casting | Cast those spells as rituals (no slots needed) |
| Expanding | Can add more ritual spells when found |

---

### Elemental Adept
*Prerequisite: Spellcasting or Pact Magic*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Choose Element | Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder |
| Bypass Resistance | Spells ignore resistance to chosen type |
| Minimum Damage | Treat 1s as 2s on damage dice of that type |
| Repeatable | Take multiple times for different elements |

---

### Spell Sniper
*Prerequisite: Spellcasting or Pact Magic*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Ability Increase | +1 spellcasting ability |
| Double Range | Spells requiring attack rolls have doubled range |
| Ignore Cover | Ignore half and three-quarters cover for spell attacks |

---

## Ability and Skill Feats

### Ability Score Improvement

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Score Increase | +2 to one ability OR +1 to two abilities |
| Maximum | Can't increase above 20 |
| Repeatable | Can take multiple times |
