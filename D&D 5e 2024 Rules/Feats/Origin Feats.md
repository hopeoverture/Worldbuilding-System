# Origin Feats

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Origin Feats are available at 1st level through your Background. Humans gain an additional Origin Feat from their Versatile trait.

---

## All Origin Feats

### Alert
*Always ready for danger.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Initiative | +5 bonus to Initiative |
| Surprise | Can't be surprised while conscious |
| Hidden Attackers | Other creatures don't gain Advantage from being hidden from you |

**Best for**: Anyone who wants to act first; controllers, damage dealers.

---

### Crafter
*Skilled artisan with efficient crafting.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Tool Proficiency | Gain proficiency with three Artisan's Tools |
| Fast Crafting | Crafting time reduced by 20% |
| Discounts | 20% discount when buying nonmagical items |

**Best for**: Downtime-focused campaigns, Artificers.

---

### Healer
*Expert at treating wounds.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Battle Medic | Use Healer's Kit to heal 1d6 + 4 + creature's number of Hit Dice |
| Healing Maximizer | When you roll dice to restore HP, treat any 1 as a 2 |

**Best for**: Non-magical healers, party support without spell slots.

---

### Lucky
*Fortune favors you.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Luck Points | 3 Luck Points, regain on Long Rest |
| Reroll | Spend 1 point to reroll any d20 (your roll or attack against you) |
| Choice | Choose which roll to use |

**Best for**: Everyone. One of the strongest feats in the game.

---

### Magic Initiate
*Learn spells from another class.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Cantrips | Learn 2 cantrips from Cleric, Druid, or Wizard list |
| 1st-Level Spell | Learn 1 first-level spell (can cast 1/Long Rest free, or with slots) |
| Ability | Choose INT, WIS, or CHA as your spellcasting ability |
| Repeatable | Can take multiple times, different list each time |

**Popular choices**:
- **Cleric**: *Guidance*, *Spare the Dying*, *Bless*/*Healing Word*
- **Druid**: *Shillelagh*, *Guidance*, *Goodberry*
- **Wizard**: *Booming Blade*, *Minor Illusion*, *Find Familiar*

**Best for**: Non-casters wanting utility; expanding spell options.

---

### Musician
*Music grants inspiration.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Instrument Proficiency | Gain proficiency with 3 musical instruments |
| Inspiring Song | After Short/Long Rest, give Heroic Inspiration to up to PB allies |

**Best for**: Bards (thematic), party support roles.

---

### Savage Attacker
*Brutal efficiency in combat.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Damage Reroll | Once per turn, reroll weapon damage dice and use either total |

**Best for**: Martial classes with high-damage weapons; great with Greataxe.

---

### Skilled
*Broad competence.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Proficiencies | Gain proficiency in 3 skills or tools of your choice |
| Repeatable | Can take multiple times, different choices each time |

**Best for**: Skill-focused characters, filling party gaps.

---

### Tavern Brawler
*Expert improvised fighter.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Unarmed Damage | Unarmed Strike deals 1d4 + STR |
| Bonus Action | After hitting with Unarmed Strike, can grapple as Bonus Action |
| Furniture Fighting | Proficient with improvised weapons, deal 1d8 |
| Reroll | Once per turn, reroll damage of Unarmed Strike or improvised weapon |

**Best for**: Monks, grapplers, tavern-themed characters.

---

### Tough
*Exceptional durability.*

| Benefit | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Hit Points | +2 HP per character level (including 1st) |
| Scaling | Continues to add +2 HP each level gained |

**Best for**: Everyone, especially low-HP classes (Wizard, Sorcerer).

## Choosing Origin Feats

### For Martial Characters
| Priority | Feat | Reason |
|----------|------|--------|
| 1st | Alert | Act first, never surprised |
| 2nd | Lucky | Protect key rolls |
| 3rd | Tough | More HP |
| 4th | Savage Attacker | More damage |

### For Spellcasters
| Priority | Feat | Reason |
|----------|------|--------|
| 1st | Lucky | Protect concentration, key saves |
| 2nd | Magic Initiate | More spells |
| 3rd | Alert | Cast control before enemies act |
| 4th | Tough | Survive hits |

### For Skill Monkeys
| Priority | Feat | Reason |
|----------|------|--------|
| 1st | Skilled | +3 proficiencies |
| 2nd | Lucky | Never fail important checks |
| 3rd | Alert | Never be caught off guard |
