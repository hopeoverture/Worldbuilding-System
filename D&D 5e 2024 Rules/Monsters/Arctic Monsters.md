# Arctic Monsters

Creatures found in frozen tundra, glaciers, ice caves, and perpetually cold regions. These monsters have adapted to survive extreme cold and often use ice, snow, and freezing temperatures as weapons or camouflage.

## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Extreme Cold:** Constitution saves or gain exhaustion
- **Blizzards:** Heavily obscured, difficult terrain
- **Thin Ice:** May break under weight
- **Avalanches:** Triggered by loud noises or combat

### Common Encounter Themes
- Yeti ambush during a blizzard
- Winter wolf pack hunt
- Frost giant raiding party
- White dragon lair assault
- Polar bear protecting cubs

### Treasure Themes
Arctic creatures often guard:
- Frozen corpses with preserved equipment
- Ice-preserved artifacts
- Frost giant crafted weapons
- White dragon hoards (cold-resistant items)
