# Coastal Monsters

Creatures found along shorelines, beaches, tidal caves, sea cliffs, and areas where land meets sea. These monsters are equally comfortable in shallow water and on land.

## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Tidal changes:** Areas flood or drain
- **Slippery rocks:** Difficult terrain, falling risk
- **Rip currents:** Strength checks to swim free
- **Storm surge:** Sudden flooding

### Common Encounter Themes
- Sahuagin raid on fishing village
- Sea hag coven in tidal caves
- Harpy nest on sea cliffs
- Dragon turtle demanding ship tribute
- Ghost ship crew seeking release

### Treasure Themes
Coastal creatures often guard:
- Shipwreck salvage
- Pearls and coral
- Pirate treasure caches
- Magical items from drowned sailors
