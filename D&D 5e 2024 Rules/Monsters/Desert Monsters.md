# Desert Monsters

Creatures found in arid wastelands, sand dunes, rocky badlands, and oasis environments. These monsters have adapted to survive extreme heat, scarce water, and shifting sands.

## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Extreme Heat:** Constitution saves or gain exhaustion
- **Sandstorms:** Heavily obscured, difficult terrain
- **Quicksand:** Restrained, sinking danger
- **Mirages:** Deception, wasted travel

### Common Encounter Themes
- Gnoll raiding party on caravan
- Mummy rising in disturbed tomb
- Blue dragon lording over oasis
- Lamia's illusory paradise trap
- Yuan-ti temple infiltration

### Treasure Themes
Desert creatures often guard:
- Ancient tomb treasures
- Genie-granted magical items
- Preserved artifacts
- Gems and precious stones
