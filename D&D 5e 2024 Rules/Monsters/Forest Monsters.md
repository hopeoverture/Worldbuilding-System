# Forest Monsters

Creatures found in woodlands, jungles, and ancient groves. The most diverse habitat, forests host everything from peaceful fey to savage predators. These monsters use trees, underbrush, and canopy for shelter, ambush, and territory.

## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Dense undergrowth:** Difficult terrain
- **Falling branches:** Dexterity saves
- **Quicksand/bog:** Restrained condition
- **Enchanted areas:** Wild magic, fey crossings

### Common Encounter Themes
- Goblin ambush on forest road
- Owlbear defending territory
- Dryad protecting sacred grove
- Green hag coven bargain
- Treant awakening to threat

### Treasure Themes
Forest creatures often guard:
- Fey-touched magical items
- Ancient elven artifacts
- Druidic relics
- Natural gems and crystals
