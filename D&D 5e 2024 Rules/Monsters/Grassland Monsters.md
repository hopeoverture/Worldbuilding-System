# Grassland Monsters

Creatures found in prairies, savannas, steppes, and open plains. These monsters are often fast-moving hunters or herd animals adapted to wide-open spaces with few hiding places.

## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Wildfires:** Spreading flames, smoke inhalation
- **Stampedes:** Trampling damage
- **Flash floods:** Sudden rushing water
- **Exposed terrain:** No cover, visible for miles

### Common Encounter Themes
- Gnoll war party raid
- Lion pride hunting
- Centaur tribal encounter
- Bulette ambush from below
- Chimera territorial defense

### Treasure Themes
Grassland creatures often guard:
- Nomad tribal artifacts
- Ancient burial mounds
- Dinosaur bones/fossils
- Trade caravan goods
