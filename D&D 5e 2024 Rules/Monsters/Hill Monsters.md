# Hill Monsters

Creatures found in hilly terrain, rolling highlands, and elevated regions below true mountains. These monsters often lair in caves, burrows, and ancient ruins scattered across the landscape.

## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Loose scree:** Difficult terrain, noise
- **Cliff faces:** Climbing, falling
- **Ancient barrows:** Undead, traps
- **Hidden caves:** Surprise encounters

### Common Encounter Themes
- Hill giant demanding tribute
- Ogre lair in ancient ruins
- Manticore hunting from crags
- Kobold warren with traps
- Dragon's hillside lair

### Treasure Themes
Hill creatures often guard:
- Ancient barrow treasures
- Giant-crafted items
- Stolen village goods
- Cave mineral wealth
