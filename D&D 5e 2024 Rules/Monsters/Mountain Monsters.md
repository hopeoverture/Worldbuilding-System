## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Thin air:** Constitution saves at high altitude
- **Avalanches:** Triggered by loud noise
- **Cliff faces:** Climbing, falling damage
- **Extreme cold:** Constitution saves

### Common Encounter Themes
- Dragon lair in volcanic peak
- Giant stronghold siege
- Griffon nest raid
- Roc hunting party prey
- Yeti ambush in snowstorm

### Treasure Themes
Mountain creatures often guard:
- Dwarven forge-works
- Giant-sized treasures
- Dragon hoards
- Ancient dwarven artifacts
