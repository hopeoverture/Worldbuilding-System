## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Elemental Planes:** Extreme temperatures, crushing pressure, suffocation
- **Outer Planes:** Alignment effects, planar traits
- **Astral Plane:** Timeless, psychic navigation
- **Ethereal Plane:** Ghostly terrain, ethereal curtains

### Common Encounter Themes
- Genie bargaining and wish corruption
- Devil contract negotiation
- Demon invasion portal defense
- Angel judgment trial
- Elemental node destabilization

### Treasure Themes
Planar creatures often guard:
- Genie lamps and binding vessels
- Infernal contracts and soul coins
- Celestial relics and holy artifacts
- Elemental gemstones and primal crystals
- Astral silver and ethereal residue

## Planar Organization

### Inner Planes (Elementals)
- **Elemental Plane of Air:** Djinni, air elementals, invisible stalkers
- **Elemental Plane of Earth:** Dao, earth elementals, xorn, galeb duhr
- **Elemental Plane of Fire:** Efreeti, fire elementals, salamanders, azers
- **Elemental Plane of Water:** Marid, water elementals

### Outer Planes (Celestials & Fiends)
- **Upper Planes:** Deva, planetar, solar, empyrean, couatl
- **Lower Planes (Hells):** Imps through pit fiends (devil hierarchy)
- **Lower Planes (Abyss):** Quasits through balors (demon chaos)
- **Neutral Evil:** Yugoloths (mercenary fiends)

### Transitive Planes
- **Astral Plane:** Astral dreadnought, githyanki
- **Ethereal Plane:** Ghosts, phase spiders
- **Shadowfell:** Shadows, sorrowsworn, nightwalkers
- **Feywild:** Fey creatures (see Forest/Grassland)
