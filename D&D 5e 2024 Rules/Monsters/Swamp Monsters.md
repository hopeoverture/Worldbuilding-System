# Swamp Monsters

Creatures found in marshes, bogs, fens, and wetlands. These monsters thrive in murky water, tangled vegetation, and areas where land and water blur together.

## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Difficult terrain:** Mud, shallow water, vegetation
- **Quicksand/deep mud:** Restrained, sinking
- **Disease:** Constitution saves from exposure
- **Poor visibility:** Fog, mist, darkness

### Common Encounter Themes
- Lizardfolk tribal village
- Hag's lair bargain
- Will-o'-wisp leading party astray
- Black dragon lair in ruins
- Shambling mound ambush

### Treasure Themes
Swamp creatures often guard:
- Sunken treasure
- Hag ritual components
- Drowned traveler belongings
- Ancient submerged ruins
