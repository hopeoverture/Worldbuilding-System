# Underdark Monsters

Creatures found in subterranean realms, deep caves, and the vast underground networks known as the Underdark. These monsters have adapted to complete darkness, scarce food, and alien ecosystems.

## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Total darkness:** Blindness without darkvision
- **Narrow passages:** Squeeze rules
- **Unstable tunnels:** Cave-ins
- **Toxic air:** Poison saves in some areas

### Common Encounter Themes
- Mind flayer colony raid
- Drow patrol ambush
- Beholder lair infiltration
- Ooze-filled corridor
- Fungal forest traverse

### Treasure Themes
Underdark creatures often guard:
- Drow magical items
- Mind flayer psionic devices
- Rare minerals and gems
- Ancient dwarven artifacts
