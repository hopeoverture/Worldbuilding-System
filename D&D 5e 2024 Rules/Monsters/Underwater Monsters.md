## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Drowning:** Breath-holding rules, suffocation
- **Pressure:** Crushing damage at extreme depths
- **Cold water:** Constitution saves in frigid seas
- **Visibility:** Darkness below sunlight zone

### Common Encounter Themes
- Sahuagin raid on coastal village
- Aboleth's underwater temple
- Kraken attack on ship
- Merfolk diplomacy mission
- Sunken treasure retrieval

### Treasure Themes
Underwater creatures often guard:
- Sunken ship cargo
- Merfolk pearls and coral crafts
- Aboleth psionic artifacts
- Ancient drowned city relics
- Dragon turtle accumulated hoards
