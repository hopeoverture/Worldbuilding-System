# Urban Monsters

Creatures found in cities, towns, and other settled areas. These monsters have adapted to living alongside humanoid civilizations, whether hidden in shadows, dwelling in sewers, or walking openly among the populace.

## Encounter Building Notes

### Environmental Hazards
- **Crowds:** Difficult terrain, witnesses
- **City watch:** Reinforcement arrival
- **Collapsing buildings:** Falling debris
- **Fire spread:** Urban conflagrations

### Common Encounter Themes
- Thieves guild confrontation
- Cult infiltration of nobility
- Sewer monster hunt
- Vampire masquerade
- Shapeshifter impersonation reveal

### Treasure Themes
Urban creatures often guard:
- Guild vault contents
- Noble house wealth
- Temple relics
- Merchant guild coffers
- Criminal enterprise profits
