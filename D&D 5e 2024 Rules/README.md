## New in 2024

### Major Changes
- **Weapon Mastery**: Martial classes unlock special properties on weapons
- **Species**: "Race" renamed to "Species" with updated traits
- **Backgrounds**: Now grant ability score increases and Origin Feats
- **Spells**: Many spells updated with clearer wording
- **Conditions**: Clarified and some updated effects
- **Feats**: Reorganized into Origin, General, Fighting Style, and Epic Boon categories

### Key Class Changes
- **Barbarian**: Brutal Strikes, Primal Knowledge
- **Bard**: Expertise at 2nd level
- **Cleric**: Divine Order (Protector or Thaumaturge)
- **Druid**: Wild Shape improvements
- **Fighter**: Weapon Mastery with more weapons
- **Monk**: Disciplines, focus on martial arts
- **Paladin**: Lay on Hands improvements
- **Ranger**: Hunter's Mark always prepared
- **Rogue**: Cunning Strikes
- **Sorcerer**: Innate Sorcery
- **Warlock**: Pact Boon at 1st level
- **Wizard**: Arcane Recovery improvements
