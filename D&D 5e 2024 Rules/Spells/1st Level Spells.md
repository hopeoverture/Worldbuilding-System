# 1st Level Spells

> [!info] Quick Reference
> 1st level spells are the foundation of spellcasting, available from level 1 for full casters and level 2 for half-casters.

---

|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | Self (15-foot cone) |

## Key 1st Level Spells Summary

| Spell | Best For | Why |
|-------|----------|-----|
| Bless | Support | +1d4 to attacks and saves for party |
| Healing Word | Emergency healing | Bonus action, ranged |
| Shield | Defense | +5 AC as reaction |
| Magic Missile | Reliable damage | Never misses |
| Faerie Fire | Anti-invisibility | Grants advantage |
| Sleep | Low-level encounters | No save, just knocks out |
| Find Familiar | Utility | Scouting, help action |
| Hex/Hunter's Mark | Damage dealing | +1d6 per hit |
