# 2nd Level Spells

> [!info] Quick Reference
> 2nd level spells become available at 3rd level for full casters and 5th level for half-casters. These spells offer significant power upgrades over 1st level options.

---

## Combat Spells

### Flaming Sphere
*Conjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 60 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (bit of tallow, pinch of brimstone, dusting of powdered iron) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard |

Create a 5-foot diameter sphere of fire. Any creature ending turn within 5 feet makes DEX save: 2d6 fire damage on failure, half on success. As Bonus Action, move sphere up to 30 feet. Can ram creatures (same damage, same save). Ignites flammables and sheds bright light 20 feet, dim light additional 20 feet.
**At Higher Levels**: +1d6 per slot level above 2nd.

### Melf's Acid Arrow
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 90 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (powdered rhubarb leaf and adder stomach) |

| Classes | Wizard |

### Moonbeam
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 120 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (several seeds of any moonseed plant and a piece of opalescent feldspar) |

## Key 2nd Level Spells Summary

| Spell | Best For | Why |
|-------|----------|-----|
| Spiritual Weapon | Clerics | Bonus action damage, no concentration |
| Hold Person | Control | Paralysis = auto-crits in melee |
| Misty Step | Mobility | Bonus action teleport |
| Pass without Trace | Stealth | +10 to entire party |
| Lesser Restoration | Healing | Removes key conditions |
| Invisibility | Infiltration | Hour-long stealth |
| Web | Control | Restrains and creates difficult terrain |
| Aid | Support | HP boost that stacks with healing |
