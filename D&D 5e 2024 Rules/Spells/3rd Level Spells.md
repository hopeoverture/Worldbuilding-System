# 3rd Level Spells

> [!info] Quick Reference
> 3rd level spells represent a significant power spike, available at 5th level for full casters. These include iconic spells like Fireball, Counterspell, and Revivify.

---

## Combat Spells

### Call Lightning
*Conjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 120 feet |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 10 minutes |
| Classes | Druid |

Create a 60-foot high, 10-foot radius storm cloud. Call down a bolt as Action (including on cast): each creature within 5 feet of a point you choose makes DEX save. 3d10 lightning damage on failure, half on success. Outdoors in a storm: 4d10 damage.
**At Higher Levels**: +1d10 per slot level above 3rd.

---

### Counterspell
*Abjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Reaction (when you see a creature within 60 ft. casting a spell) |
| Range | 60 feet |
| Components | S |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard |

Interrupt a creature casting a spell. If target casting 3rd level or lower, spell fails. If higher level, make ability check (DC 10 + spell's level) using your spellcasting ability.
**At Higher Levels**: Automatically counters spell of equal or lower level.

---

### Dispel Magic
*Abjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 120 feet |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard |

End one spell on target creature, object, or area. If spell is 3rd level or lower, it ends. If higher, make ability check (DC 10 + spell's level) using your spellcasting ability.
**At Higher Levels**: Automatically dispels spell of equal or lower level.

## Utility Spells

### Animate Dead
*Necromancy*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 minute |
| Range | 10 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (drop of blood, piece of flesh, pinch of bone dust) |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Cleric, Wizard |

Create zombie from Medium/Small corpse or skeleton from pile of bones. Undead follows your verbal commands. On your turn, you can mentally command any creature you animated (no action if within 60 feet). You must reassert control within 24 hours or it becomes uncontrolled.
**At Higher Levels**: Animate or reassert control over +2 undead per slot level above 3rd.

---

### Bestow Curse
*Necromancy*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | Touch |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Bard, Cleric, Wizard |

Touch creature, which makes WIS save. On failure, choose one curse:
- Disadvantage on checks/saves with one ability score
- Disadvantage on attack rolls against you
- Must make WIS save at start of turn or waste Action
- Your attacks/spells deal extra 1d8 necrotic damage

**At Higher Levels**: 4th—8 hours, no concentration. 5th—24 hours. 7th—until dispelled.

## Key 3rd Level Spells Summary

| Spell | Best For | Why |
|-------|----------|-----|
| Fireball | Damage | 8d6 in 20-foot radius |
| Counterspell | Control | Stop enemy spellcasting |
| Revivify | Healing | Bring back the dead |
| Spirit Guardians | Clerics | Sustained damage aura |
| Hypnotic Pattern | Control | Incapacitate multiple targets |
| Haste | Buff | Double speed, extra action |
| Fly | Mobility | 60-foot flying speed |
| Dispel Magic | Utility | End magical effects |
