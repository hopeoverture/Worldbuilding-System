# 4th Level Spells

> [!info] Quick Reference
> 4th level spells become available at 7th level for full casters and 13th level for half-casters. These spells offer powerful combat options and transformative effects like Polymorph.

---

## Combat Spells

### Banishment
*Abjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 60 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (item distasteful to target) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Cleric, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard |

Target creature makes CHA save. On failure:
- **Native to current plane**: Banished to harmless demiplane, incapacitated. Returns when spell ends.
- **Not native**: Banished to home plane. If concentration maintained for full duration, doesn't return.

**At Higher Levels**: +1 creature per slot level above 4th.

### Blight
*Necromancy*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 30 feet |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard |

Necromantic energy drains moisture from target. CON save: 8d8 necrotic damage on failure, half on success. Plant creatures have disadvantage on save and take maximum damage. Nonmagical plant that isn't a creature withers and dies.
**At Higher Levels**: +1d8 per slot level above 4th.

### Fire Shield
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | Self |
| Components | V, S, M (bit of phosphorus or firefly) |
| Duration | 10 minutes |
| Classes | Wizard |

Flames wreathe your body, shedding bright light 10 feet, dim light additional 10 feet. Choose:
- **Warm Shield**: Resistance to cold damage. Creatures hitting you with melee attack take 2d8 fire damage.
- **Chill Shield**: Resistance to fire damage. Creatures hitting you with melee attack take 2d8 cold damage.

### Wall of Fire
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 120 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (small piece of phosphorus) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard |

Create wall up to 60 feet long, 20 feet high, 1 foot thick, or a 20-foot diameter, 20-foot high ringed wall (both sides of ring face inward). One side (your choice) deals damage. ## Defensive/Healing Spells

*Abjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|

| Range | Self (30-foot radius) |

### Freedom of Movement
*Abjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | Touch |
| Components | V, S, M (leather strap, bound around arm or leg) |
| Duration | 1 hour |

### Confusion
*Enchantment*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 90 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (three nut shells) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard |

Each creature in 10-foot-radius sphere makes WIS save. On failure, affected creature rolls d10 at start of each turn:

- **2-6**: Take no action/movement

### Polymorph
*Transmutation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 60 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (caterpillar cocoon) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 hour |
| Classes | Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard |

Transform creature into new beast form. Unwilling target makes WIS save. Can't affect shapechanger or creature at 0 HP. Target's game statistics replaced by beast's stats, but retains alignment and personality. HP becomes beast's HP. When reverts (drops to 0 HP or spell ends), returns with HP it had before transformation. Limited to beasts with CR ≤ target's level (or CR if no level). Can't cast spells. Actions limited to those of new form.

**Popular Beast Forms:**

| Form | CR | HP | Notes |
|------|----|----|-------|
| Giant Ape | 7 | 157 | Strong attacks, climbing |
| Tyrannosaurus Rex | 8 | 136 | Massive damage |
| Mammoth | 6 | 126 | Trampling charge |
| Giant Elk | 2 | 42 | Good mobility |

## Key 4th Level Spells Summary

| Spell | Best For | Why |
|-------|----------|-----|
| Polymorph | Versatility | Transform ally into T-Rex or Giant Ape |
| Banishment | Control | Remove extraplanar creatures permanently |
| Greater Invisibility | Combat advantage | Attack while invisible |
| Dimension Door | Escape/utility | 500-foot teleport with ally |
| Wall of Fire | Area denial | Sustained damage, blocks movement |
| Death Ward | Protection | Prevents death once |
| Freedom of Movement | Utility | Immune to movement restrictions |
| Fire Shield | Defense | Damage resistance + retaliation |
