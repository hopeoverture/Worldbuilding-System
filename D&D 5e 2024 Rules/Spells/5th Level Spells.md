# 5th Level Spells

> [!info] Quick Reference
> 5th level spells become available at 9th level for full casters and represent the peak of half-caster spell access. These include powerful effects like Wall of Force, Raise Dead, and Hold Monster.

---

## Combat Spells

### Cloudkill
*Conjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 120 feet |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 10 minutes |
| Classes | Sorcerer, Wizard |

20-foot-radius sphere of poisonous fog. Area heavily obscured. Spreads around corners. Creature entering area for first time or starting turn there makes CON save: 5d8 poison damage on failure, half on success. Fog moves 10 feet away from you at start of each turn, rolling along ground. Sinks into openings.
**At Higher Levels**: +1d8 per slot level above 5th.

## Control/Utility Spells

### Animate Objects
*Transmutation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 120 feet |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard |

Animate up to 10 nonmagical objects. Each makes melee attack using your spell attack bonus.

| Size | HP | AC | Attack | Damage |
|------|----|----|--------|--------|
| Tiny | 20 | 18 | +8 | 1d4+4 |
| Small | 25 | 16 | +6 | 1d8+2 |
| Medium | 40 | 13 | +5 | 2d6+1 |
| Large | 50 | 10 | +6 | 2d10+2 |
| Huge | 80 | 10 | +8 | 2d12+4 |

Count by size: 2 Small = 1 Medium, 2 Medium = 1 Large, 2 Large = 1 Huge.
**At Higher Levels**: +2 objects per slot level above 5th.

### Bigby's Hand
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 120 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (eggshell and snakeskin glove) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Wizard |

Create Large spectral hand (AC 20, HP = your max HP, STR 26, DEX 10). Moves up to 60 feet and acts using Bonus Action. Choose:
- **Clenched Fist**: Melee spell attack, 4d8 force damage
- **Forceful Hand**: Push creature within 5 feet (STR check vs. spell DC), push 5 feet + 5 × modifier
- **Grasping Hand**: Grapple (escape DC = spell DC), 2d6+spellcasting modifier bludgeoning while grappled
- **Interposing Hand**: Half cover from hand's position
**At Higher Levels**: Clenched Fist +2d8, Grasping Hand +2d6 per slot level above 5th.

## Key 5th Level Spells Summary

| Spell | Best For | Why |
|-------|----------|-----|
| Wall of Force | Control | Impenetrable barrier, can trap enemies |
| Hold Monster | Control | Paralysis works on any creature |
| Raise Dead | Restoration | Bring back dead party members |
| Greater Restoration | Healing | Cures exhaustion, petrification, curses |
| Animate Objects | Damage | Action economy, swarm attacks |
| Telekinesis | Utility | Move creatures and objects at will |
| Synaptic Static | Damage + debuff | AoE psychic + attack penalty |
| Destructive Wave | Paladin AoE | 10d6 damage + prone in radius |
