# 6th Level Spells

> [!info] Quick Reference
> 6th level spells become available at 11th level for full casters. These represent truly powerful magic, including healing entire parties, instant death effects, and permanent transformations.

---

## Combat Spells

### Chain Lightning
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 150 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (bit of fur, piece of amber/glass/crystal rod, three silver pins) |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Sorcerer, Wizard |

Bolt leaps to primary target, then arcs to up to 3 additional targets within 30 feet of the first. Each target makes DEX save: 10d8 lightning damage on failure, half on success. Each target can only be struck once.
**At Higher Levels**: +1 target per slot level above 6th.

### Disintegrate
*Transmutation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 60 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (lodestone and pinch of dust) |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Sorcerer, Wizard |

Thin green ray. DEX save: 10d6+40 force damage on failure, nothing on success. Creature reduced to 0 HP is disintegrated (only True Resurrection or Wish can restore). Can target Large or smaller nonmagical object or section of object up to 10-foot cube, disintegrating it. Instantly destroys force effects like Wall of Force.
**At Higher Levels**: +3d6 per slot level above 6th.

### Sunbeam
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | Self (60-foot line) |
| Components | V, S, M (magnifying glass) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard |

60-foot line, 5 feet wide. Each creature makes CON save: 6d8 radiant damage and blinded until your next turn on failure, half damage and not blinded on success. Undead and oozes have disadvantage. | Casting Time | 10 minutes |

## Control/Utility Spells

### Arcane Gate
*Conjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 500 feet |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 10 minutes |
| Classes | Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard |

Create two linked portals (10-foot diameter, 2D circles). One within 10 feet of you, one within 500 feet. Any creature or object entering one portal exits the other instantly. Can rotate either portal (facing any direction) as Bonus Action.

### Contingency
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 10 minutes |
| Range | Self |
| Components | V, S, M (ivory statuette of yourself worth 1,500+ GP) |
| Duration | 10 days |
| Classes | Wizard |

Store a spell of 5th level or lower (casting time 1 Action, can target you) that automatically casts when a circumstance you describe occurs. Describe circumstance clearly. Contingent spell takes effect immediately when triggered. Can only have one Contingency active.

**Popular Contingencies:**
- "If I drop to 0 HP" → Dimension Door
- "If I'm paralyzed" → Freedom of Movement
- "If I fall" → Feather Fall

### Create Undead
*Necromancy*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 minute |
| Range | 10 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (150 GP black onyx per corpse) |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Cleric, Warlock, Wizard |

Create 3 ghouls from Medium or Small humanoid corpses. Only castable at night. Undead obey verbal commands. Must reassert control within 24 hours or become uncontrolled.
**At Higher Levels**: 7th—4 ghouls or 2 ghasts/wights. 8th—5 ghouls, 3 ghasts/wights, or 2 mummies. 9th—6 ghouls, 4 ghasts/wights, or 3 mummies.

### Eyebite
*Necromancy*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | Self |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard |

Gaze at creature within 60 feet. WIS save. On failure, choose one (use on new target each turn as Action):
- **Asleep**: Falls unconscious (wakes if damaged or Action used to wake it)
- **Panicked**: Frightened, must Dash away from you

### Flesh to Stone
*Transmutation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 60 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (pinch of lime, water, and earth) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |

### Globe of Invulnerability
*Abjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | Self (10-foot radius) |
| Components | V, S, M (glass or crystal bead) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Sorcerer, Wizard |

10-foot-radius sphere. Any spell of 5th level or lower cast from outside can't affect creatures/objects within or target anything inside. Spells can be cast from inside to outside normally.
**At Higher Levels**: Blocks spell level equal to slot level - 1.

---

### Mass Suggestion
*Enchantment*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 60 feet |
| Components | V, M (snake's tongue and honeycomb) |
| Duration | 24 hours |
| Classes | Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard |

Up to 12 creatures that can hear and understand you make WIS save. On failure, pursue a reasonable course of action (1-2 sentences) you suggest. Obviously harmful suggestions fail. Spell ends when activity completed.
**At Higher Levels**: Duration increases—7th = 10 days, 8th = 30 days, 9th = 1 year.

*Transmutation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 120 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (iron blade and small bag of soil) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 2 hours |

Reshape terrain in 40-foot square up to 10 feet in any dimension over 10 minutes. Can create/fill trenches, raise/lower elevation, create slopes. At 10-minute intervals, can affect additional areas. Doesn't damage structures. Creatures in area move with terrain.

## Key 6th Level Spells Summary

| Spell | Best For | Why |
|-------|----------|-----|
| Heal | Healing | 70 HP instant heal |
| Heroes' Feast | Preparation | Immunity to fear/poison, extra HP |
| Disintegrate | Damage | 10d6+40, destroys Wall of Force |
| Mass Suggestion | Control | 12 targets, 24-hour duration |
| True Seeing | Detection | Truesight 120 feet |
| Chain Lightning | AoE damage | 10d8 to 4 targets |
| Contingency | Defense | Auto-trigger escape/protection |
