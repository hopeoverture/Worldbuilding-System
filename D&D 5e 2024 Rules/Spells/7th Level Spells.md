# 7th Level Spells

> [!info] Quick Reference
> 7th level spells become available at 13th level for full casters. These are world-shaping spells including Resurrection, Teleport, and Plane Shift.

---

## Combat Spells

### Delayed Blast Fireball
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 150 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (tiny ball of bat guano and sulfur) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Sorcerer, Wizard |

Create a bead of light that can be detonated or waits until spell ends. DEX save: 12d6 fire damage on failure, half on success. For each round the bead waits, damage increases by 1d6 (max 22d6). If touched before detonation, creature makes DEX save—failure detonates it. 20-foot radius.
**At Higher Levels**: +1d6 initial damage per slot level above 7th.

### Divine Word
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Bonus Action |
| Range | 30 feet |
| Components | V |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Cleric |

Any creature that can hear you within range makes CHA save. Effect based on current HP:

| Current HP | Effect |
|------------|--------|
| 50 or fewer | Deafened for 1 minute |
| 40 or fewer | Deafened and blinded for 10 minutes |
| 30 or fewer | Blinded, deafened, and stunned for 1 hour |
| 20 or fewer | Killed instantly |

Celestials, elementals, fey, or fiends that fail are banished to home plane for 24 hours (can't return unless via Wish).

### Prismatic Spray
*Evocation*

|--------|--------|

| Range | Self (60-foot cone) |
| Components | V, S |

Eight rays of light flash. Each creature in 60-foot cone rolls d8:

|------|-------|--------|
| 1 | Red | 10d6 fire damage (DEX save half) |
| 2 | Orange | 10d6 acid damage (DEX save half) |

## Healing/Restoration Spells

### Regenerate
*Transmutation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 minute |
| Range | Touch |
| Components | V, S, M (prayer wheel and holy water) |
| Duration | 1 hour |
| Classes | Bard, Cleric, Druid |

Creature regains 4d8+15 HP. For duration, regains 1 HP at start of each turn (10 HP per minute). Severed body parts regrow after 2 minutes (if held against stump, reattaches instantly).

---

### Resurrection
*Necromancy*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 hour |
| Range | Touch |
| Components | V, S, M (diamond worth 1,000+ GP, consumed) |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Bard, Cleric |

Return creature (dead up to 100 years) to life with full HP. Neutralizes poisons, cures nonmagical diseases, lifts curses. Closes mortal wounds but doesn't restore missing body parts. Target has -4 penalty to attacks, saves, and checks (reduced by 1 each long rest). Can't resurrect undead or creatures that died of old age.

## Control/Utility Spells

### Etherealness
*Transmutation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | Self |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Up to 8 hours |
| Classes | Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard |

Step into Ethereal Plane. Can see into Material Plane (50 feet, shades of gray). Can only affect/be affected by creatures on Ethereal. Move in any direction, pass through solid objects on Material (take 1d10 force damage if you end turn inside object). Return to Material as Action.
**At Higher Levels**: +3 creatures per slot level above 7th.

---

### Forcecage
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 100 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (ruby dust worth 1,500 GP) |
| Duration | 1 hour |
| Classes | Bard, Warlock, Wizard |

Create immobile, invisible prison. Choose:
- **Cage**: 20-foot cube with ½-inch bars, ½-inch gaps. Spells can pass through.
- **Box**: 10-foot cube with solid walls. Blocks spells except those that can cross planes.

Creature inside can't leave by nonmagical means. If creature tries to teleport/plane shift out, must make CHA save or fail. Can't be dispelled.

### Mirage Arcane
*Illusion*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 10 minutes |
| Range | Sight |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | 10 days |
| Classes | Bard, Druid, Wizard |

Make terrain in 1-mile square look, sound, smell, and feel like another terrain type. General shape of terrain stays the same. Structures can be added or removed. Creatures with truesight see both versions superimposed. Physical interaction: tactile elements are real enough to affect creatures (illusory quicksand restrains, illusory cliff can be climbed).

### Plane Shift
*Conjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | Touch |
| Components | V, S, M (forked rod worth 250+ GP, attuned to destination plane) |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard |

You and up to 8 willing creatures teleport to a different plane. Destination is general (within 5-50 miles of intended location). Can also banish unwilling creature: melee spell attack, creature makes CHA save or transported to random location on plane you choose.

### Simulacrum
*Illusion*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 12 hours |
| Range | Touch |
| Components | V, S, M (snow/ice, plus powdered ruby worth 1,500 GP, consumed) |
| Duration | Until dispelled |
| Classes | Wizard |

Create duplicate of a beast or humanoid within range. Has half the creature's max HP and lacks ability to learn or become stronger. Obeys your commands. If reduced to 0 HP, reverts to snow. Can repair with 100 GP per HP (8 hours work). Can only have one simulacrum at a time.

### Symbol
*Abjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 minute |
| Range | Touch |
| Components | V, S, M (mercury, phosphorus, powdered diamond and opal worth 1,000+ GP, consumed) |
| Duration | Until dispelled or triggered |
| Classes | Bard, Cleric, Wizard |

Inscribe harmful glyph on surface or in object. When triggered (creature within 60 feet or opening/damaging object), all creatures within 60 feet affected (WIS save for most). Choose effect: Death (10d10 necrotic), Discord (argue 1 minute), Fear (frightened, flee), Hopelessness (despair, can't attack), Insanity (can't communicate, random actions), Pain (incapacitated), Sleep, or Stunning.

### Teleport
*Conjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 10 feet |
| Components | V |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard |

You and up to 8 willing creatures (or one object up to 10-foot cube) teleport to destination on same plane. Accuracy depends on familiarity:

| Familiarity | On Target | Off Target | Similar Area | Mishap |
|-------------|-----------|------------|--------------|--------|
| Permanent circle | — | — | — | — (auto) |
| Associated object | — | — | — | — (auto) |
| Very familiar | 01-97 | 98-99 | 100 | — |
| Seen casually | 01-88 | 89-94 | 95-98 | 99-100 |
| Viewed once | 01-76 | 77-88 | 89-96 | 97-100 |
| Description | 01-49 | 50-73 | 74-88 | 89-100 |
| False destination | — | — | 01-50 | 51-100 |

**Mishap**: 3d10 force damage, roll again.

## Key 7th Level Spells Summary

| Spell | Best For | Why |
|-------|----------|-----|
| Teleport | Travel | Instant transportation across plane |
| Plane Shift | Planar travel | Travel between planes |
| Resurrection | Restoration | Return dead to life after 100 years |
| Forcecage | Control | Inescapable prison |
| Etherealness | Infiltration | Pass through solid matter |
| Prismatic Spray | Damage | Devastating random effects |
| Divine Word | Combat | Instant kill vs. low HP targets |
| Simulacrum | Utility | Create duplicate of creature |
