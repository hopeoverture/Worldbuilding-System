# 8th Level Spells

> [!info] Quick Reference
> 8th level spells become available at 15th level for full casters. These are legendary spells capable of shaping reality, creating demiplanes, and permanently transforming creatures.

---

## Combat Spells

### Dominate Monster
*Enchantment*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 60 feet |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 hour |
| Classes | Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard |

Creature makes WIS save (advantage if you or allies fighting it). On failure, charmed, telepathic link. Can command it (no action) or use Action for total control. Can repeat save when damaged. Save is made with +5 bonus if commanded against its nature.
**At Higher Levels**: 9th level = 8-hour duration.

### Earthquake
*Evocation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 500 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (pinch of dirt, rock, and clay) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 minute |
| Classes | Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer |

100-foot-radius area. When cast and at end of each turn you maintain concentration, each creature on ground in area makes DEX save or knocked prone. Area becomes difficult terrain. Fissures open—10% chance per 10 feet moved, d10 depth (1-6 = 10 feet, 7-9 = 20 feet, 10 = chasm). Structures take 50 bludgeoning damage per round; if reduced to 0 HP, collapse.

### Feeblemind
*Enchantment*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 150 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (handful of clay, crystal, glass, or mineral spheres) |
| Duration | Instantaneous |
| Classes | Bard, Druid, Warlock, Wizard |

Blast creature's mind. INT save: 4d6 psychic damage on success. On failure: 4d6 psychic damage AND INT and CHA become 1, can't cast spells, can't understand language, can't activate magic items, and speech is incomprehensible. Can repeat save every 30 days. ## Defensive/Utility Spells

### Antimagic Field
*Abjuration*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | Self (10-foot radius sphere) |
| Components | V, S, M (pinch of powdered iron or iron filings) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 1 hour |
| Classes | Cleric, Wizard |

10-foot radius sphere of antimagic. Spells can't be cast inside, summoned creatures vanish, magic items become mundane, spell effects are suppressed. Doesn't affect artifacts or deities.

**Key Interactions:**
- Dispel Magic: Can't dispel the field
- Targeted Spells: Blocked if either caster or target is inside
- Areas of Effect: Suppressed inside the field
- Magic Items: Become mundane while inside
- Summoned Creatures: Vanish while inside (return when field moves)
- Teleportation: Blocked into or out of field

### Control Weather
*Transmutation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 10 minutes |
| Range | Self (5-mile radius) |
| Components | V, S, M (burning incense and bits of earth and wood mixed in water) |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 8 hours |
| Classes | Cleric, Druid, Wizard |

Control weather in 5-mile radius. Takes 1d4 × 10 minutes for new conditions to take effect. Can change precipitation, temperature, and wind. Each change can shift conditions one stage toward your goal.

| Stage | Precipitation | Temperature | Wind |
|-------|---------------|-------------|------|
| 1 | Clear | Unbearable heat | Calm |
| 2 | Light clouds | Hot | Moderate |
| 3 | Overcast/ground fog | Warm | Strong |
| 4 | Rain/snow/hail | Cool | Gale |
| 5 | Torrential rain/driving snow/blizzard | Cold | Storm |

## Transformation Spells

### Animal Shapes
*Transmutation*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 Action |
| Range | 30 feet |
| Components | V, S |
| Duration | Concentration, up to 24 hours |
| Classes | Druid |

Transform up to 10 willing creatures into beasts (CR ≤ 4 for each). Each can have different form. As Bonus Action, transform all affected creatures into new forms. Retain alignment and personality; HP becomes beast's; at 0 HP, reverts with excess damage carrying over.

### Antipathy/Sympathy
*Enchantment*
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Casting Time | 1 hour |
| Range | 60 feet |
| Components | V, S, M (alum soaked in vinegar for antipathy, or honey for sympathy) |
| Duration | 10 days |
| Classes | Druid, Wizard |

Target a Huge or smaller object/area up to 200-foot cube. Specify a kind of creature. Choose:

**Antipathy**: Creatures of specified kind must make WIS save when within 60 feet. On failure, frightened while in range and must flee. Can repeat save every 24 hours.

**Sympathy**: Creatures of specified kind within 60 feet must make WIS save. On failure, must move toward target and stay within range. Can repeat save every 24 hours.

| Range | 60 feet |

## Key 8th Level Spells Summary

| Spell | Best For | Why |
|-------|----------|-----|
| Mind Blank | Protection | Immune to psychic damage, charm, divination |
| Clone | Immortality | Backup body for resurrection |
| Dominate Monster | Control | Charm any creature type |
| Feeblemind | Debuff | INT/CHA = 1, can't cast spells |
| Antimagic Field | Counter-magic | Suppress all magic in area |
| Power Word Stun | Control | No save stun if ≤150 HP |
| Demiplane | Utility | Create private extradimensional space |
| Holy Aura | Buff | Advantage on saves, disadvantage on attacks vs. allies |
