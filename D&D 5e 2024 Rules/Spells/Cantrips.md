# Cantrips

> [!info] Quick Reference
> Cantrips are 0-level spells that can be cast at will without expending spell slots. Most damage cantrips scale at levels 5, 11, and 17.

---

## Cantrip Basics

### Key Rules
- Cast unlimited times per day
- Don't use spell slots
- Damage scales with character level (not class level)
- Known cantrips are typically fixed (can't swap easily)

### Scaling
| Character Level | Damage Dice |
|-----------------|-------------|
| 1-4 | 1 die |
| 5-10 | 2 dice |
| 11-16 | 3 dice |
| 17+ | 4 dice |

## Cantrip Recommendations

### Best Damage Cantrips by Role

| Role | Recommended | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Ranged DPR | Eldritch Blast | Highest damage, multiple beams |
| Melee | Shocking Grasp | Stops reactions, allows retreat |
| Versatile | Fire Bolt | Good range, solid damage |
| Debuff | Vicious Mockery | Disadvantage on attacks |
| Anti-Healing | Chill Touch | Stops regeneration |

### Best Utility Cantrips

| Cantrip | Strength |
|---------|----------|
| Guidance | +1d4 to any ability check |
| Mage Hand | Ranged manipulation |
| Minor Illusion | Versatile distraction |
| Prestidigitation | Countless creative uses |
| Light | Free light source |

### Cantrips by Class

| Class | Must-Have | Good Options |
|-------|-----------|--------------|
| Bard | Vicious Mockery | Minor Illusion, Mage Hand |
| Cleric | Toll the Dead, Guidance | Sacred Flame, Spare the Dying |
| Druid | Guidance, Produce Flame | Thorn Whip, Shillelagh |
| Sorcerer | Fire Bolt | Mind Sliver, Prestidigitation |
| Warlock | Eldritch Blast | Minor Illusion, Mage Hand |
| Wizard | Fire Bolt, Minor Illusion | Mage Hand, Prestidigitation |
