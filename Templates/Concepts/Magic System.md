## Image Prompts

### Art Style
**Hyper-realistic digital art, photorealistic rendering, cinematic lighting, rich textures, intricate detail on magical effects and arcane imagery, dramatic atmosphere, depth of field, volumetric lighting, 8K quality, masterful composition.**

### Guidelines
When generating AI art for this magic system, include:
- **System identity:** The visual signature that distinguishes this magic from others—its characteristic colors, patterns, and manifestations
- **Practitioner investment:** The cost, concentration, and skill visible in casting—magic as achievement rather than gift
- **Reality interaction:** How the magic changes the world around it—not just effects but ambient response
- **Source suggestion:** Visual hints at where the power comes from—arcane symbols for learned magic, nature motifs for primal, divine light for granted
- **Limitation evidence:** The constraints and costs that make the system interesting rather than omnipotent
- **Tradition markers:** The tools, gestures, and trappings specific to this magical practice

### Magic System Integration (Magic Systems Are Power Made Comprehensible)
Every element should express the system's rules and the experience of using it:
- *Don't write:* "wizard casting spell" → *Write:* "an arcanist at the moment of breakthrough, the formula she's worked on for months finally resolving—the geometric patterns of pure magic forming around her hands as understanding and power merge, the air thick with ozone and the taste of copper, her eyes glowing with the strain of holding forces that could unmake her if she loses concentration"
- *Don't write:* "magical energy flowing" → *Write:* "the ley-current visible for once, a river of power flowing beneath the earth's surface, rising where the standing stones channel it—the energy the green-gold of spring growth, carrying the scent of growing things, the plants around the nexus flowering out of season, responding to power older than humanity"
- *Don't write:* "magic circle on floor" → *Write:* "the summoning circle drawn in silver-infused chalk that's worth its weight in gold, the geometry precise to tolerances a human hand can barely achieve, each symbol referencing centuries of magical theory—the circle glowing not because the spell has begun but because such accumulated understanding creates its own resonance"

### Distinctive Feature Ideas for Magic Systems
- **Source visualization:** Where the power comes from made visible—ley lines, divine radiance, personal aura
- **Practice evidence:** The tools and techniques specific to this tradition—geometric circles, wild gestures, precise words
- **Cost manifestation:** The price of power visible—exhaustion, material consumption, reality strain
- **Effect signature:** The characteristic way this magic looks—color palette, geometric vs organic forms, sound and smell
- **Mastery gradient:** How novice casting differs from expert work—rough vs refined, dangerous vs controlled
- **Environmental response:** How the world reacts to magic being used—temperature changes, light shifts, temporal distortions
