# {{title}}

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> When generating a trap, create a dangerous obstacle that challenges players without feeling arbitrary or unfair. Use D&D 5e (2024) trap rules: Traps are either Nuisance (unlikely to seriously harm) or Deadly (can grievously damage). Include clear triggers, detection methods, disabling options, and logical placement. The best traps tell a story about who placed them and why.

## Overview
> Write 2-3 sentences establishing this trap's nature—what it does, how dangerous it is, and why it exists in this location.
