## Image Prompts

### Art Style
**Hyper-realistic digital art, photorealistic rendering, cinematic lighting, rich textures, intricate detail on foliage and bark, dramatic atmosphere, depth of field, volumetric lighting, 8K quality, masterful composition.**

### Guidelines
When generating AI art for this forest, include:
- **Canopy character:** Light penetration, density, height, tree species silhouettes
- **Depth layering:** Foreground detail fading to mysterious middle and distant layers
- **Ground life:** Ferns, fungi, moss, fallen logs, leaf litter, animal tracks
- **Light quality:** Dappled sunlight, misty rays, twilight glow, shadowed depths
- **Atmosphere:** Humidity, mist, dust motes, pollen, seasonal character
- **Signs of life:** Movement glimpsed, sounds implied, paths worn by passage

### Forest Integration (Forests Are Living Cathedrals)
Every element should suggest the forest's age, character, and hidden life:
- *Don't write:* "tall trees" → *Write:* "ancient oaks rising like pillars in a cathedral of green, their trunks wider than a man's armspan, bark furrowed deep as canyons with centuries of growth, their canopy so far above that the first branches begin where lesser trees would end"
- *Don't write:* "forest floor" → *Write:* "the ground a soft carpet of centuries—last year's leaves over older decay over the bones of fallen giants, mushrooms rising in fairy rings where trees died decades past, ferns unfurling where shafts of light pierce the canopy"
- *Don't write:* "sunlight through trees" → *Write:* "light filtering through the leaves in shafts of dusty gold, each beam alive with drifting pollen and tiny insects, illuminating small clearings while leaving the spaces between in green shadow"

### Distinctive Feature Ideas for Forests
- **Age markers:** ancient hollow trees, nurse logs with saplings growing from them, lightning scars healed over centuries, root systems exposed by erosion
- **Life abundance:** bracket fungi on every dead trunk, bird nests visible in winter, deer trails worn into the soil, claw marks on bark from climbing animals
- **Forest architecture:** natural arches where roots bridge streams, cathedral spaces beneath old growth, thickets impenetrable to humans
- **Atmosphere makers:** mist hanging in hollows, fog caught in branches, autumn leaves mid-fall, snow dusting upper branches while ground remains bare
- **Magical touches (if applicable):** trees that grow in impossible shapes, phosphorescent fungi, flowers that bloom in moonlight, paths that seem to shift
