## Image Prompts

### Art Style
**Hyper-realistic digital art, photorealistic rendering, cinematic lighting, rich textures, intricate detail on historical scenes and period elements, dramatic atmosphere, depth of field, volumetric lighting, 8K quality, masterful composition.**

### Guidelines
When generating AI art for this event, include:
- **Moment crystallization:** The instant when history pivoted—captured at the point of maximum drama and significance
- **Participant truth:** People experiencing the event as they would have—with appropriate emotion, confusion, fear, or triumph
- **Environmental witness:** The setting responding to what's happening—fire reflecting on faces, crowds pressing, weather affecting action
- **Period authenticity:** Clothing, equipment, and surroundings accurate to the era and culture
- **Consequence foreshadowing:** Visual hints of what this moment will mean—the death that changes everything, the arrival that transforms
- **Scale revelation:** The scope of the event made visible—intimate personal tragedy or continental upheaval

### Event Integration (Events Are History Made Immediate)
Every element should express the moment's significance and its power to reshape the world:
- *Don't write:* "assassination scene" → *Write:* "the instant before the blade reaches the king—the assassin's arm already descending, the bodyguards turning too late, the courtiers frozen between denial and horror, the king's own face showing the recognition that comes just before death, the crown beginning to tilt as his body jerks away, every detail sharp because this moment ends an era and begins a war"
- *Don't write:* "natural disaster" → *Write:* "the eruption that would bury the city in a single night—the mountain's throat opening in fire and smoke, the first wave of ash already falling on the marketplace where people have stopped to stare at something their grandparents warned about but no one truly believed, the harbor visible in the distance where the only chance of survival waits, the entire scene lit by hellfire from above"
- *Don't write:* "peace declaration" → *Write:* "the herald reading the treaty terms to a crowd that has known only war since their parents' childhood—faces showing the strange mixture of relief and uncertainty, children who have never known peace pressing forward to hear words they don't understand, the banner of the old enemy flying beside their own for the first time, the silence of a world learning to stop fighting"

### Distinctive Feature Ideas for Events
- **Frozen motion:** The instant before consequence—arrows in flight, blades descending, words leaving lips
- **Crowd psychology:** The emotional range of witnesses—believers and skeptics, beneficiaries and victims
- **Environmental response:** Fire, water, earth, and air participating in the drama
- **Symbolic elements:** Objects or arrangements that will later become the event's visual shorthand
- **Human scale:** Individuals within larger events—the single face in the crowd, the one who ran while others watched
- **Time markers:** Light, weather, and wear that establish when in the day, season, or era this occurs
