## Image Prompts

### Art Style
**Hyper-realistic digital art, photorealistic rendering, cinematic lighting, rich textures, intricate detail on legendary materials and magical effects, dramatic atmosphere, depth of field, volumetric lighting, 8K quality, masterful composition.**

### Guidelines
When generating AI art for this artifact, include:
- **Impossible presence:** Materials, craftsmanship, or properties that transcend normal making—the sense that gods or powers beyond mortals had a hand
- **Age beyond measure:** The weight of centuries or millennia, yet preserved beyond natural possibility
- **Power barely contained:** The artifact's might should feel restrained, leashed, waiting—suggesting capabilities far beyond what's visible
- **Reality distortion:** Subtle wrongness in how light behaves, how shadows fall, how the air moves near the artifact
- **Legendary materials:** Substances that shouldn't exist or can no longer be made—star-metal, titan-bone, crystallized divine essence
- **Destiny weight:** The sense that this object matters, that history bends around its presence

### Artifact Integration (Artifacts Are Power Made Object)
Every element should express the artifact's world-shaping significance and terrible potential:
- *Don't write:* "ancient magical crown" → *Write:* "the Crown of Seven Dominions, forged when the world was young from metals that fell from the sky, each of its seven points topped with a gem that contains the bound essence of a conquered demon-prince—the crown that made the First Empire possible and unmade its last emperor when he proved unworthy"
- *Don't write:* "glowing orb on pedestal" → *Write:* "the Orb of Annihilation resting in a containment circle drawn in blood that must be renewed each dawn, the sphere's surface not quite reflecting the light around it but seeming to drink it, the air above it wavering like heat-shimmer though the touch is cold—cold like the space between stars, cold like the end of everything"
- *Don't write:* "ancient sword" → *Write:* "the Worldsplitter in its case of blessed stone, the blade that carved the strait between continents when a demigod's anger demanded expression, the steel still singing at a frequency below hearing, the edge sharp enough to cut concepts as well as flesh—sheathed now, but the sheath vibrates constantly with the effort of containment"

### Distinctive Feature Ideas for Artifacts
- **Impossible materials:** Substances that don't exist naturally, can no longer be made, or behave in ways that defy understanding
- **Power manifestation:** The artifact's might made visible—light that moves wrong, shadows that behave independently, temperature anomalies
- **History scars:** The marks of legendary events—the crater where it struck the earth, the staining where god-blood touched it
- **Reality distortion:** Subtle wrongness in the artifact's vicinity—distances that seem different than they measure, time that flows strangely
- **Containment evidence:** The wards, circles, cases, or guardians that suggest the artifact's danger even at rest
- **Destiny weight:** The sense that important things happen around this object, that it draws fate toward itself
