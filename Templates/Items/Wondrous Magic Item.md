## Image Prompts

### Art Style

### Guidelines
When generating AI art for this wondrous item, include:
- **Purpose in form:** The item's shape and design revealing its magical function—divination items mirror-like, protection items solid and encompassing
- **Magic made visible:** The enchantment manifest in the physical—not just applied but inherent to the item's nature
- **Craft tradition:** The magical school or culture evident in style—elvish items organic and flowing, dwarven items geometric and precise
- **Material significance:** Components chosen for magical sympathy—moonstones for illusion, dragon scales for fire, etc.
- **Use evidence:** The marks of activation, the wear patterns of magical operation
- **Reality relationship:** How the item interacts with the mundane world—floating slightly, always warm, casting wrong shadows

### Wondrous Item Integration (Magic Items Are Enchantment Made Tangible)
Every element should express the marriage of craft and magic that created the item:
- *Don't write:* "magical cloak" → *Write:* "a cloak that seems woven from twilight itself, the fabric shifting between deep purple and star-pricked black depending on the light, the edges never quite staying still, as if the hem exists in a breeze that touches nothing else—the clasp a crescent moon in silver that's always cool, the whole garment weighing less than it should and draping in ways that hide the wearer's true outline"
- *Don't write:* "glowing amulet" → *Write:* "an amulet of protection forged in the old way, the metal a silver-iron alloy that hasn't been made in centuries, the guardian's eye carved in sapphire at its heart seeming to blink at the edges of vision—the chain sized to rest the eye over the heart, the weight exactly right to remind the wearer of what watches over them without ever being burden"
- *Don't write:* "magical ring" → *Write:* "a Ring of Spell Storing worn thin by centuries of use, the band etched with channels that glow faintly when charged, currently holding three spells visible as different colored lights moving slowly through the metalwork like blood through veins—the band warm with borrowed magic, humming at a frequency felt rather than heard"

### Distinctive Feature Ideas for Wondrous Items
- **Function form:** Design that reveals purpose—scrying mirrors perfectly reflective, flying carpets impossibly light
- **Magic pulse:** The visible rhythm of enchantment—glows that breathe, colors that cycle, warmth that waxes and wanes
- **Material magic:** Components that carry inherent power—phoenix feather cores, titan-blood ink, moonsilver thread
- **Activation markers:** The evidence of magical use—channels that light when triggered, gems that brighten, surfaces that shift
- **Craft identity:** The magical tradition visible in style—academic arcana precise and geometric, wild magic organic and asymmetric
- **Reality friction:** The ways the item doesn't quite fit the mundane world—wrong shadows, odd temperatures, uncomfortable angles
