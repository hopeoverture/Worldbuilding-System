> When prompting AI for world maps, use these key terms:
> - "Top-down view" or "bird's eye view" for perspective
> - "Fantasy cartography" or "D&D world map" for style
> - "Parchment texture" or "aged paper" for background
> - Specify terrain representation: "mountain symbols," "forest textures"
> - Include "compass rose," "scale bar," "ornate border" for cartographic elements
> - Use "clean labeling areas" to leave space for text
