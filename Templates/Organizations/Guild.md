## Image Prompts

### Art Style
**Hyper-realistic digital art, photorealistic rendering, cinematic lighting, rich textures, intricate detail on craftsmanship and guild elements, dramatic atmosphere, depth of field, volumetric lighting, 8K quality, masterful composition.**

### Guidelines
When generating AI art for this guild, include:
- **Trade identity:** Tools, materials, products of the craft prominently displayed, the hall designed around the work
- **Institutional weight:** Architecture that speaks of wealth, permanence, and collective power
- **Hierarchy visible:** Guildmaster's position, member areas, apprentice spaces, the structure made physical
- **Accumulated tradition:** Guild records, historic pieces, symbols refined over generations, the weight of custom
- **Economic power:** Evidence of transactions, sample goods, the mechanisms of monopoly
- **Professional pride:** Quality of display, craft standards visible, the guild as guarantor of excellence

### Guild Integration (Guilds Are Trades Made Institutions)
Every element should express the guild's control over its craft and protection of its members:
- *Don't write:* "guild hall with banners" → *Write:* "the great hall of the guild, three stories of dressed stone and carved wood, the guild banner hanging from the hammer-beam ceiling—the crossed hammers and crown that every smith in the kingdom has sworn to uphold, woven in gold thread on field of iron grey, unchanged for three centuries"
- *Don't write:* "craftsmen at work" → *Write:* "masters of the trade gathered at the long table, their guild pins marking rank and specialty, examining a journeyman's masterwork with the critical eye of those who have spent lifetimes distinguishing good from adequate, each wearing the scars and calluses of their particular craft"
- *Don't write:* "trade samples displayed" → *Write:* "the sample cabinet where the guild's standards are defined—the reference pieces against which all work is judged, locked behind glass with three keys held by three masters, unchanged since the guild's founding, the physical definition of 'acceptable quality'"

### Distinctive Feature Ideas for Guilds
- **Trade trophies:** The masterwork that won the last competition, the contract for the royal commission, the first sale receipt
- **Institutional architecture:** The guildmaster's chair larger and older than the rest, the council table's seating order, the door only masters may use
- **Record keeping:** Ledgers of every transaction, rolls of members living and dead, the archives that document the trade
- **Quality mechanisms:** Testing equipment, sample cabinets, the stamps and seals that certify work
- **Economic infrastructure:** The strong room, the guild bank, the treasury that backs member credit
- **Ritual spaces:** Where oaths are sworn, where disputes are resolved, where the dead are remembered
