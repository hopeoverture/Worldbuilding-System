## Image Prompts

### Art Style
**Hyper-realistic digital art, photorealistic rendering, cinematic lighting, rich textures, intricate detail on religious vestments and architecture, dramatic atmosphere, depth of field, volumetric lighting, 8K quality, masterful composition.**

### Guidelines
When generating AI art for this religious order, include:
- **Sacred architecture:** Spaces designed to inspire reverence, to connect mortal and divine, to express the deity's nature in stone and light
- **Vestment hierarchy:** Clergy distinguished by the richness and style of their robes, rank visible in fabric and adornment
- **Ritual focus:** The altar as center, the implements of worship, the sacred objects that mediate between worlds
- **Divine symbolism:** The deity's symbols woven through every element, the visual language of faith
- **Accumulated devotion:** Centuries of prayer, worn stones, smoke-stained ceilings, the physical evidence of generations of worship
- **Community of faith:** Clergy and congregation united in practice, the living institution rather than empty architecture

### Religious Order Integration (Faith Is Community Made Visible)
Every element should express the order's beliefs, practices, and spiritual authority:
- *Don't write:* "priests in robes" → *Write:* "the hierarchy of the faith arranged in the sanctuary, each rank distinguished by vestment—the acolytes in simple white, the priests in cloth-of-gold, the high priest in robes that cost a village's yearly income, embroidered with scenes from the sacred texts by blind nuns who work by touch alone"
- *Don't write:* "sacred altar" → *Write:* "the altar that has stood since the faith's founding, the marble stained dark by five centuries of offerings, the eternal flame burning in its bronze basin since the first priest lit it from the god's own fire—no other flame may touch this one, and if it ever dies, the faith believes the world ends with it"
- *Don't write:* "religious symbols" → *Write:* "the sun-and-wheel of the Radiant Lord appearing everywhere—cast in gold above the altar, woven in silver on the vestments, carved in the pews where congregants rest their hands, painted on the windows where it catches the dawn light and scatters it in rainbows across the sanctuary"

### Distinctive Feature Ideas for Religious Orders
- **Vestment distinction:** Colors and materials marking rank, ceremonial versus daily wear, the visible hierarchy of holiness
- **Sacred objects:** The relics in their cases, the implements of ritual, the objects that connect to divine power
- **Ritual evidence:** Candle wax layered on holders, incense smoke staining stone, the worn places where knees have pressed for generations
- **Architectural theology:** Windows positioned for sacred light, acoustics designed for chanting, the building as theological statement
- **Living practice:** Clergy in the midst of ritual, congregation at worship, the order as functioning community
- **Temporal markers:** The bells that call to prayer, the calendar of feast and fast, the rhythm of sacred time
