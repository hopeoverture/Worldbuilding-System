# Campaign: The Awakening Shadows

> [!info] Campaign Summary
> A Level 1-12 campaign for the world of **Valdoria**.
> **Premise:** The ancient evil known as **The Imprisoned** is stirring beneath the Sundered Peaks. The **Covenant of the Unveiled Truth** works to break the ancient seals binding it. The heroes must uncover the conspiracy, gather the keys to the prison, and defeat the Avatar of Shadow before it escapes.

## Act 1: The Early Shadow (Levels 1-4)
*In which local disturbances reveal a global conspiracy.*

### Main Adventures
- **Level 1:** [[The Whispering Caves]]
    - *Summary:* Rescue mission in a goblin-infested mine revealing the first **Shard of Shadow**.
- **Level 2:** [[The Silent Bell]]
    - *Summary:* Investigation of a corrupted priest in Oakhaven; the first encounter with shapeshifters.
- **Level 3:** [[Ruins of the Dawn]]
    - *Summary:* A race against mercenaries to recover the **Dawn Key** from an ancient spire.
- **Level 4:** [[The Traitors Masquerade]]
    - *Summary:* A high-stakes heist at a noble's gala to expose **Lord Valerius**.

### Interludes
- **Level 1:** [[The Mourning Wake]]
    - *Summary:* Defending Oakhaven's cemetery from restless dead.
- **Level 2:** [[The Crossed Roads]]
    - *Summary:* A bandit ambush reveals Covenant recruitment tactics.
- **Level 3:** [[Riverboat to Highwall]]
    - *Summary:* A murder mystery aboard the *Gilded Swan* en route to the capital.
- **Level 4:** [[The Stolen Supplies]]
    - *Summary:* Recovering essential gear from the Thieves' Guild before the wilderness journey.

---

## Act 2: The Growing Darkness (Levels 5-9)
*In which the scale of the threat becomes undeniable and open war begins.*

### Main Adventures
- **Level 5:** [[Shadows in the Wood]]
    - *Summary:* Cleansing the **Whisperwood** of corruption feeding the mountains.
- **Level 6:** [[The Forgotten Shrine]]
    - *Summary:* A trial of worthiness to earn the **Sunstone** from a Sphinx.
- **Level 7:** [[Siege of the Grey Keep]]
    - *Summary:* Commanding the defense of a fortress against a Covenant army.
- **Level 8-9:** [[Into the Sundered Peaks]]
    - *Summary:* A deadly crossing of the mountains involving survival and an Ancient White Dragon.

### Interludes
- **Level 5:** [[The Bridge of Bones]]
    - *Summary:* A haunted crossing guarding the Valdris Vale.
- **Level 6:** [[The Refugee Caravan]]
    - *Summary:* Escorting civilians through a war zone.
- **Level 7:** [[The Scouts Report]]
    - *Summary:* Hunting an Invisible Stalker to recover strategic maps.
- **Level 9:** [[The Ice Worms Tunnel]]
    - *Summary:* A dangerous shortcut through a Remorhaz lair to bypass a storm.

---

## Act 3: The Breaking of Seals (Levels 10-12)
*In which the heroes face the end of the world.*

### Main Adventures
- **Level 10:** [[The Obsidian Gate]]
    - *Summary:* Infiltrating the fortress of the Imprisoned to disable its defenses.
- **Level 11-12:** [[Echoes of the First Shadow]]
    - *Summary:* The final battle against the **Avatar of the Imprisoned** to reseal the prison.

### Interludes
- **Level 10:** [[The Void Breach]]
    - *Summary:* Surviving a reality-warping hallway before the final confrontation.
