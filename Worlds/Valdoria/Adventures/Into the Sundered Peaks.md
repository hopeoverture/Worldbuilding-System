## The Hook

### How It Begins
The party learns that the Covenant's destination is the **Obsidian Gate**, dealing with the Imprisoned directly. The only way there is through the **Dragon's Spine**.

### Act 1: The Ascent
**Goal:** Survive the elements.
**Hazards:** Avalanches, Blizzards (Exhaustion checks), Remorhaz attacks.

**Goal:** Navigate the Ice Caves.
**Key Scenes:**
1.  **Frozen Victims:** Statues of previous adventurers (warning).

**Goal:** Deal with the Dragon.
**Climactic Encounter:** **Cryonax** (Adult White Dragon stats, buffed with Shadow Lair actions).
