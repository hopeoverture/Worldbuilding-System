> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A roleplay-heavy interlude dealing with the journey to the capital.

## Overview
To reach **Highwall** in time for the Masquerade, the party takes a fast riverboat, **The Gilded Swan**. During the night, a courier carrying evidence against the Covenant is murdered. The party must find the killer among the passengers before the boat docks.

## Classification
**Adventure Type:** Whodunit
**Tone:** Noir / Suspense

## The Hook
**Captain Orivan** asks the party for help when a passenger is found dead in their cabin, locked from the inside.

## Background
 The victim, **Inspector Hark**, was bringing proof of Valerius's smuggling to the King. The killer is **Madame Vane**, a surprisingly spry elderly noblewoman (actually a Covenant assassin).

## Objectives
### Primary Objective
Identify the killer and recover the evidence.

## Structure
### Act 1: The Body
Investigating the crime scene. Finding the poisoned needle.

### Act 2: The Interrogations
Interviewing the suspects:
1.  **The Merchant:** Nervous, smuggling wine (Red Herring).
2.  **The Bard:** Too curious for their own good.
3.  **Madame Vane:** Feigns shock.

### Act 3: The Reveal
Confronting Vane. She tries to escape by jumping overboard or fighting.
**Enemy:** **Assassin** (nerfed for lvl 3) or **Spy** stats.

## Rewards
- **The Evidence:** Gives advantage in the social encounters in Adventure 4.
