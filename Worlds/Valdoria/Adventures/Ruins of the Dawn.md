## Overview
Following the trail from Oakhaven, the adventurers race to the **Ruins of the Dawn**, an ancient Architect observatory hidden in the foothills. Their goal is to intercept a **Covenant Retrieval Team** led by the ruthless mercenary **Captain Varrick**. The ruins hold a **Seal-Key**, a relic needed to reinforce the weakening magical barriers. The party must navigate ancient defenses, outmaneuver the cultists, and secure the Key before it falls into enemy hands.

## Background

### The Situation
Captain Varrick has set up a base camp outside the ruins and is drilling through the outer door. His mage consultant is deciphering the wards. They haven't breached the inner sanctum yet.

### History
- **3000 years ago:** Built by the Architects to monitor the Prison seals.
- **500 years ago:** Abandoned during the Fall of the Old Kingdoms.
- **Yesterday:** Varrick's team arrived.

## Adventure Structure

### Act 1: The Approach
**Goal:** Infiltrate or assault the excavation site.

**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Camp:** Varrick has 10 guards. The party can sneak past, cause a distraction, or fight.
2.  **The Entry:** The outer door is breached just as the party arrives/enters.

**Information to Reveal:**
- Varrick is working for a noble named "Lord V." (Valerius).

**Transition to Act 2:** Entering the Ruins.

### Act 2: The Gauntlet
**Goal:** Race through the dungeon faster than the cultists.

**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Prism Hall:** A puzzle involving aligning mirrors to open a light-door.
2.  **The Collapsed Bridge:** Crossing a chasm while under fire from cultist crossbowmen on the other side.
3.  **The Construct:** An **Ancient Defender** (animated armor/helmed horror) activates and attacks both sides.

**Rising Tension:** Tremors shake the ruins as the Consultant tries to force the inner door.

**Midpoint Twist:** The ruins begin to self-destruct/flood with light energy due to the forced entry.

**Transition to Act 3:** Reaching the Inner Sanctum.

### Act 3: The Prize
**Goal:** Boss battle for the Key.

**Key Scenes:**
1.  **Sanctum Showdown:** The Key is on a pedestal. Varrick is there.
2.  **The Escape:** fleeing the crumbling spire.

**Climactic Encounter:** Three-way battle: Party vs. Varrick vs. The Guardian System (Lair Actions).

**Resolution Options:**
- Varrick fights to the death.
- If reduced to low HP, he might barter info for his life.
