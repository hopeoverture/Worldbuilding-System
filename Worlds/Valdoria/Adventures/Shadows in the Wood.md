## The Hook

### How It Begins
The party is met at the forest edge by **Lirael**, a dryad whose bark is grey and cracking. She collapses, begging for help to save "The Heart Tree."

### Initial Information
- The forest is "sick." Animals are rabid.
- A black sludge flows through the rivers.

## Background

### The Situation
**Malakor**, a druid seduced by the Covenant's promises of "rebirth," is performing a rituals at the three Ley Nodes. He has already corrupted two.

### Key NPCs' Goals
| NPC | Goal | Method |
|-----|------|--------|

## Objectives

### Primary Objective
Cleanse the three Ley Nodes and defeat Malakor.

### Secondary Objectives
- Save the remaining Unicorn (it's being hunted).

## Adventure Structure

### Act 1: The Rot
**Goal:** Reach the first Node.

**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Dead River:** Crossing a river of sludge. **Black Puddings** attack.
2.  **The Corrupted Bear:** A **Cave Bear** (mutated) attacks.

### Act 2: The Hunt
**Goal:** Reach the Heart Tree.

**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Stalking:** The party is hunted by **Displacer Beasts** leading a pack of corrupted wolves.
2.  **The Choice:** Save a burning grove or pursue the villain.

### Act 3: The Heart
**Goal:** Stop the ritual.

**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Heart Tree:** A massive tree bleeding black ichor.
2.  **Malakor's Transformaton:** He merges with the rot.

**Climactic Encounter:** Malakor (Druid of Spores/Rot) + **Shambling Mound** (Corrupted).
