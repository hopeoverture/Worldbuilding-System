## Overview
To enter the **Valdris Vale**, one must cross the **Bridge of Bones**, an ancient structure said to be made from the ribs of a fallen titan. The bridge is haunted by the **Grief of Ages**. The party cannot cross by force; they must pay a toll of memory.

## Classification
**Adventure Type:** Social / Puzzle
**Tone:** Melancholic

## The Hook
The bridge is shrouded in mist. Those who step on it find themselves walking forever without reaching the other side.

## Objectives
### Primary Objective
Break the loop and cross the bridge.
