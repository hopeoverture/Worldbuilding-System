# {{title}}

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A travel encounter bridging the journey to the ruins.

## Overview
En route to the **Ruins of the Dawn**, the party finds a blocked bridge on the **Traveler's Way**. A group of bandits, the "Red Bandanas," usually just demand a toll. Today, they are demanding "offerings for the Mask." They have been radicalized by a Covenant recruiter.

## Classification

### Type
**Adventure Type:** Social / Combat

**Tone:** Gritty

### Scope
**Estimated Sessions:** One-Shot (1-2 Hours)

**Party Level:** 2

## The Hook
A fallen tree blocks the road. "Toll collectors" step out, but they are carving symbols into their arms.

## Background
**Bandit Captain Jorry** accepted gold from **Captain Varrick** to delay any travelers heading to the ruins.

## Objectives
### Primary Objective
Pass the roadblock.

### Secondary Objective
Learn about Varrick's head start.

## Adventure Structure

### Act 1: The Parley
**Goal:** Talk or Fight.
**Social:** Jorry is unstable. A high Persuasion check might make him realize he's being used.

### Act 2: The Fight
**Goal:** Clear the road.
**Enemies:**
- **Bandit Captain** (Jorry).
- 4 **Bandits**.
- 1 **Cultist** (The handler, hiding in the bushes).

## Rewards
- **Loot:** 50gp, and a map showing Varrick's camp location (Advantage on survival checks in Adventure 3).
