> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A dangerous shortcut.

## Overview
To bypass the unnatural storm guarding the Obsidian Gate, the party uses the maps found earlier to locate an old **Remorhaz** hibernation tunnel. It's warm, but it's also occupied.

## Classification
**Adventure Type:** Dungeon
**Tone:** Claustrophobic

## The Hook
The storm above is impassable. The cave mouth below emits heat.

## Objectives
### Primary Objective
Traverse the tunnel without waking the **Mother Worm**.

## Structure
### Act 1: The Eggs
Navigating a chamber filled with Remorhaz eggs. Stealth is key.

### Act 2: The Hatchlings
Combat with **Young Remorhaz**.

### Act 3: The Exit
A chase sequence as the Mother wakes up.

## Rewards
- **Surprise:** Coming out *inside* the Obsidian Gate's perimeter wall.
