## Overview
The party stands before the **Obsidian Fortress**, a massive structure built by the Architects to guard the **Prison of the Imprisoned**, now occupied and fortified by the Covenant. The final seal is breaking. To stop the ritual, the party must infiltrate the fortress, sabotage the four **Elemental Lodestones** powering the barrier override, and open the path to the inner chamber.
