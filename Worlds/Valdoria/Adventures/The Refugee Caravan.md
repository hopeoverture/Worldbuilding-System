# {{title}}

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A war-themed escort mission.

## Overview
War has broken out. As the party travels to the **Grey Keep**, they encounter a caravan of refugees fleeing the Covenant's advance guards. The caravan is slow and being tracked by **Worg Riders**. The party must defend them until they reach safety.

## Classification
**Adventure Type:** Escort / Defense
**Tone:** Desperate

## The Hook
A burning wagon on the road. Screams in the distance.

## Objectives
### Primary Objective
Ensure at least 50% of the wagons reach the Keep.

## Structure
### Act 1: The Rear Guard
Fighting off a pack of **Worgs** harassing the stragglers.

### Act 2: The Broken Wheel
A wagon breaks down. Decision: Fix it (lose time), abandon it (lose supplies), or scatter the loot.

### Act 3: The Ambush
**Hobgoblin Captain** sets up a roadblock.

## Rewards
- **Reputation:** The common folk love the party.
- **Reinforcements:** The refugees include a blacksmith who can repair gear at the Keep (Adventure 7).
