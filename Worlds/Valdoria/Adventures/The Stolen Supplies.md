# {{title}}

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A logistics-focused interlude.

## Overview
Before heading into the dangerous **Whisperwood**, the party orders special alchemical supplies (Anti-toxins, Silvered weapons). However, the shipment is stolen by the **Rat's Nest** gang just before pickup. The party must track them down to the docks and recover the gear.

## Classification
**Adventure Type:** Urban Skirmish
**Tone:** Gritty / Street Level

## The Hook
The Alchemist **Vex** apologizes; his delivery boy was mugged.

## Background
The gang doesn't know the importance of the goods; they just want to fence them.

## Objectives
### Primary Objective
Recover the crate of supplies marked "Vex".

## Structure
### Act 1: The Chase
Tracking the thieves through the crowded market (Urban tracking / Intimidation).

### Act 2: The Warehouse
Infiltrating the gang's hideout.
**Enemies:**
- **Thugs** (Level 4 equivalent).
- **Wererat** (Gang leader).

## Rewards
- **The Supplies:** 4x *Potions of Poison Resistance* (Essential for Adventure 5).
- **Bonus:** 200gp from the gang's stash.
