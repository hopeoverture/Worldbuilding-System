# High King Thrain IV

> [!info] Generation Instructions

**Traits:** Stubborn, Unwavering, Grim.
**Ideal:** Honor. A wrong must be righted. A debt must be paid.
**Bond:** The Tombs of the Ancestors.

## Background
Thrain has been king for 150 years. He has seen the strength of his people bleed away in the dark. He blames the Humans for not helping during the last Great Siege.

## Combat/Abilities
A juggernaut on the battlefield. Wields the hammer *Earth-Shaker*.
