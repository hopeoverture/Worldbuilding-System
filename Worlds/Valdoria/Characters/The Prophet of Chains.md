# The Prophet of Chains

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> The primary antagonist's lieutenant. A charismatic but utterly insane cult leader who hears the whispers of the Shackled God. He believes breaking the world is an act of mercy.

## Appearance
A figure wrapped in rags and rusted chains that seem to move on their own. His skin is pale as death, covered in scarified runes. His eyes are solid black pools.

## Personality
**Traits:** Fanatical, Charismatic, Terrifying.
**Ideal:** Liberation. The only freedom is in potential; existence is a cage.
**Bond:** The Shackled God.
**Flaw:** Insanity. He acts on visions that may not be real.

## Background
Once a High Priest of the Silver Flame named Malakor, he ventured too deep into the Citadel of Tears and gazed upon the First Seal. It broke his mind and filled it with the Void. Now he seeks to "free" everyone from the pain of life.

## Combat/Abilities
Wields high-level necrotic magic. Can summon chains from the ground to bind enemies.

## Plot Hooks
1.  **The Sermon:** The party stumbles upon a secret meeting where the Prophet is converting a village.
2.  **The Ritual:** The Prophet is preparing a ritual to break a minor seal; he must be stopped.
