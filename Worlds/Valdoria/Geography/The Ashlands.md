## Overview
The Ashlands form the southern peninsula of [[Thondorian]], a rugged land shaped by the fury of the earth. Dominated by active volcanoes and desolate lava fields, it is nonetheless inhabited by a hardy people who farm the mineral-rich ash soil and mine rare obsidian and gems. It is a dangerous, beautiful place of fire and black sand beaches, known for master smiths and strange fire magic.
