## Overview
The Eastern Frontier is the catch-all term for the wild lands east of [[The Sundered Peaks]] and outside the [[The Whisperwood]]. It is a land of opportunity and lawlessness, where petty warlords carve out fiefdoms and adventurers seek lost ruins. No king rules here; protection comes from steel and stone walls.
