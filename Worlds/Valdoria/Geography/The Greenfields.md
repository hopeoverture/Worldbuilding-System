## Overview
The Greenfields are a vast ocean of grass in the heart of western [[Thondorian]]. Stretching for hundreds of miles, this region is the breadbasket of the continent and the home of the famed "Horse Lords." It is a land of big skies, thundering hooves, and golden harvests, where the wind never stops blowing and freedom is valued above all else.
