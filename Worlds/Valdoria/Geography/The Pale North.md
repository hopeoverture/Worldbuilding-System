## Overview
The Pale North is a harsh, unforgiving expanse of ice and snow at the top of the world. It is a land of eternal winter, where the sun barely rises for half the year and the aurora borealis dances in the sky. Home to hardy Raider Clans, frost giants, and ancient ice-bound horrors, it is the anvil upon which the strongest warriors of [[Thondorian]] are forged.
