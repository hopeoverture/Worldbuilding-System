## Overview
The Valdris Vale is the civilized heart of [[Thondorian]], a wide, fertile valley carved by the mighty [[Valdris]] River. Flanked by the [[The Greenfields]] to the south and hills to the north, it is the center of trade, agriculture, and high culture. Rich city-states and small kingdoms line the riverbanks, united by the "River Compact" to ensure free trade. It is a land of castles, stone bridges, and manicured fieldsâ€”the safest place in a dangerous world.

### Political Status
**Type:** Confederation of River Kingdoms

## Culture

### Regional Identity
Cosmopolitan, mercantile, and slightly arrogant. They view themselves as the pinnacle of civilization.

### Religion
**Dominant Faith:** [[Yavandil]] (Harvest) and [[Silmarwen]] (Knowledge/Magic).

### Art & Architecture
Gothic stone architecture, stained glass, beautiful gardens.

## History

### Origins
Civilization grew here first due to the fertile soil and water access.

### Major Events
- **The Signing of the Compact:** Ended centuries of river wars.
- **The Great Flood:** 50 years ago, reshaped the lower valley.

## Dangers

### Creatures
- **River Trolls:** Under bridges.

### Hostile Forces
