## Overview
The Westmark is a lush, fertile region defined by rolling coastal hills, abundant rainfall, and the constant rhythm of [[The Vast Sea]]. As the most densely populated area of [[Thondorian]], it is a land of ancient maritime traditions, bustling port cities, and stable feudal kingdoms. The air here tastes of salt and rain, and the people are known for their pragmatism, ship-craft, and deep connection to the ocean.
