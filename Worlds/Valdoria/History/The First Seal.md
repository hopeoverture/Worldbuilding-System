# The First Seal

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A high-level dungeon crawl into the heart of the enemy stronghold. The party discovers that the "First Seal" binding the Shackled God is about to break. They must race to reinforce it.

## Overview
The [[Prophet of Chains]] has been located. He is in the depths of **Amon Nirnaeth**, performing the Rite of Unbinding. The party must assault the fortress, fight through levels of undead and cultists, and stop the ritual before the First Seal cracks and unleashes a localized cataclysm.

## Classification

### Type
**Adventure Type:** Dungeon Crawl / Boss Rush
**Tone:** Epic / Desperate
**Themes:**
- Ancient Evil
- Divine Duty

## The Hook

### Quest Giver
**Name:** **Grand Master Tybalt** (Order of the Silver Flame)
**Motivation:** Prevent the awakening of the Shackled God.
**What He Offers:** Legendary weapons from the Order's vault.

## Objectives

### Primary Objective
Interrupt the **Rite of Unbinding** and defeat the Prophet (or drive him off).

### Secondary Objectives
1.  Rescue captured paladins being used as sacrifices.
2.  Collapse the tunnel leading to the Underdark/Iron Holds.

## Adventure Structure

### Act 1: The Ascent
**Goal:** Breach the Citadel.
**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Weeping Bridge:** A narrow bridge under fire from gargoyles.
2.  **The Gatehouse:** A brutal fight against an Undead Giant.

### Act 2: The Depths
**Goal:** Descend to the Seal Chamber.
**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Hall of Vigils:** Fighting possessed statues.
2.  **The Prison:** Rescuing the sacrifices.

### Act 3: The Chamber of the Seal
**Goal:** The Boss Fight.
**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Ritual:** The Prophet is protected by a shield; the party must destroy 3 pylons to lower it.
2.  **The Echo:** A fragment of the Shackled God manifests.

## Key NPCs
- **boss:** **The Prophet of Chains** (High Priest).
- **Minion:** **The Shadow-Hulk** (Abomination).

## Rewards
- **Title:** "Seal-Keeper."
- **Item:** *Sunblade*.

## Image Prompts
### Key Scene
**Prompt:** A massive underground chamber lit by violet rift energy. A glowing seal on the floor is cracking. Cultists chant in a circle. Heroes charge down the stairs to stop them. Epic magic effects.
