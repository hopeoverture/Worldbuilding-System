# The Siege of Highwall

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A desperate defense mission. An Orc horde from the Ashlands, led by a Cult sorcerer, smashes against the fortress town. The party must command defenses, root out saboteurs, and hold the line.

## Overview
The "Monster Raids" were a distraction. The true army of the [[The Ashlands]] has arrived, bearing siege engines of black iron. [[Commander Kaelen]] is injured in the first assault. The party must take command of the defense and keep the gate sealed until reinforcements arrive from Rivergard (3 days away).

## Objectives

### Primary Objective
Hold the **Highwall Gate** for 3 days until reinforcements arrive.

### Secondary Objectives
1.  Identify and stop the Black Lotus saboteurs trying to open the gate from within.
2.  Destroy the Orcs' "Hell-Cannon" siege engine.

## Adventure Structure

### Act 1: The First Wave
**Goal:** Survive the initial assault.
**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Walls:** Commanding archers and repelling ladders.
2.  **The Breach:** A section of the wall cracks; the party must hold the gap.

### Act 2: The Enemy Within
**Goal:** Find the saboteurs.
**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Poisoned Well:** The water supply is targeted.
2.  **The Tunnel:** Fighting Lotus assassins in the Undercroft.

### Act 3: The Final Push
**Goal:** The enemy commander takes the field.
**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Duel:** The Orc Warlord challenges the party's champion.
2.  **The Arrival:** The horns of Rivergard sound at dawn on the 4th day.

## Key NPCs
