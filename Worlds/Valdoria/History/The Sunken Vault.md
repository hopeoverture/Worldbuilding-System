# The Sunken Vault

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A heist adventure set in Rivergard. The party is hired to retrieve a specific seal from the Submerged Library before a rival faction gets it.

## Overview
[[Grand Mayor Lysandra]] has learned that the Black Lotus is close to finding the **Seal of Tides**, an ancient artifact that controls the river's flow. If they get it, they can flood the city at will. The party must dive into the Undercity, navigate the Sunken Library, and steal the Seal first.

## Classification

### Type
**Adventure Type:** Heist / Investigation
**Tone:** Mystery / Swashbuckling
**Themes:**
- Greed vs. Duty
- The Past Haunting the Present

### Scope
**Estimated Sessions:** 2-3 Sessions
**Party Level:** 5

## The Hook

### Quest Giver
**Name:** [[Grand Mayor Lysandra]]
**Motivation:** Protect her city (and her profits) from Black Lotus extortion.
**What She Offers:** 5,000 gold pieces and a letter of marque.

### Initial Information
- The Black Lotus has found a secret entrance to [[The Sunken Library]].
- They are looking for the Seal of Tides.
- The entrance is somewhere in the "Drowning Wards."

## Objectives

### Primary Objective
Retrieve the **Seal of Tides** from the vault before the Black Lotus.

### Secondary Objectives
1.  Map the entry route for the Explorers League (Bonus Reward).
2.  Rescue a kidnapped scholar forced to help the Lotus.

## Adventure Structure

### Act 1: The Drowning Wards
**Goal:** Locate the Lotus's excavation site.
**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Information Broker:** Dealing with a slimy informant in a sinking tavern.
2.  **The Ambush:** Lotus thugs try to silence the party.

### Act 2: Into the Depths
**Goal:** Navigate the Sunken Library.
**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Librarian:** Avoiding the giant construct that guards the stacks.
2.  **The Flooded Hall:** An underwater combat with aquatic ghouls.

### Act 3: The Vault
**Goal:** The final confrontation.
**Key Scenes:**
1.  **The Puzzle Lock:** Opening the vault using First Age lore.
2.  **The Showdown:** [[The Nightmaster]]'s lieutenant arrives just as the vault opens.

## Encounters

### Boss Encounter: The Lotus Lieutenant
**Location:** The Vault Antechamber.
**Enemies:**
- **Varek the Knife (Assassin)**
- 4 Lotus Thugs
- 1 Water Elemental (Summoned)
**Tactics:** Varek uses the water elemental to separate the party while he strikes from the shadows.

## Rewards
- **Gold:** 5,000 gp.
- **Item:** *Cloak of the Manta Ray* (found in the library).
- **Favor:** The Grand Mayor owes the party a favor.

## Image Prompts
### Key Scene
**Prompt:** Adventurers wading waist-deep through a flooded library corridor. Glowing blue runes on the walls provide the only light. A dark shape swims beneath the water's surface. Suspenseful, cinematic.
