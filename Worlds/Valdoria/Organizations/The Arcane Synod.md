# The Arcane Synod

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A regulatory body for magic users. Magic is "uncommon" and "feared" in Valdoria, so this organization is as much a prison/monitor as it is a school. They are the "good guys" who do questionable things to keep the world safe.

## Overview
The Arcane Synod is the primary magical authority in [[Thondorian]]. Headquartered in the coastal tower of Thalassar, they dispatch "Watchers" to every major kingdom to identify children with magical talent. These children are taken to the Spire to be trainedâ€”and to ensure they do not succumb to the corruption that often accompanies arcane power.

## Structure
**Leader:** [[Archmage Vylara]], a centuries-old Half-Elf who remembers the last time unsupervised magic nearly broke the world.
**Branches:**
1.  **The Librarians:** Hoard and study ancient texts.
2.  **The Inquisitors:** Hunt down rogue mages ("Hedge-Witches") and dark cultists.
3.  **The Artificers:** Create magical items for the Synod's use.

## Influence
**Public Perception:** People fear the Synod but call them when magical beasts or curses appear. They are seen as a necessary evil.
**Relations:**
- **[[The Kingdom of Aerendor]]:** Tense. The King dislikes their interference but needs their help against the North.
- **[[The River Compact]]:** Good. The Synod banks with the Compact.

## Plot Hooks
1.  **The Rogue Apprentice:** A talented student has fled the Spire with a forbidden tome; the PCs are hired to catch them before the Inquisitors do (and kill them).
2.  **The Anti-Magic Riots:** A Synod tower in [[Lond Daer]] is under siege by a mob blaming them for a recent plague.
