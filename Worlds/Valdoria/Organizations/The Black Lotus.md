# The Black Lotus

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A powerful criminal syndicate that operates behind the scenes of the River Compact. They deal in smuggling, extortion, and assassination. They have a code of honor but are ruthless.

They say if you drop a coin in the Valdris River, the Black Lotus takes a copper's share before it hits the bottom. This shadowy organization controls the docks, the back alleys, and the black markets of [[The River Compact]].
