# The Order of the Silver Flame

> [!info] Generation Instructions
> A militant religious order dedicated to a deity of light/justice. They are the primary hunters of the "Dark Cults" and undead. They can be zealous and rigid.

## Overview
Clad in silver plate and bearing shields emblazoned with a white flame, the Knights of the Order are a symbol of hope to the peasantry and a headache to kings. They answer only to their Grand Master and their God. They wander the land hunting corruption, often dispensing summary justice.

## Structure
**Leader:** [[Grand Master Tybalt]]. A scarred veteran who sees demons in every shadow.
**Ranks:** Initiate, Squire, Knight-Brother, Paladin, Justicar.

## Plot Hooks
1.  **The Witch Hunt:** The Order arrives in a village identifying a local healer as a witch; the party knows she is innocent.
2.  **The Cleansing:** The Order hires the party to help clear a crypt that is too dangerous even for them.
